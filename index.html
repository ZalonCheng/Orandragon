9. Alternative Investment

Reading 50 Introduction to Alternative Investment

1. Introduction

Investment Classification

  • Traditional investment
  • Alternative investment

Alternatives Characteristics

  • 高杠杆高风险高收益
  • Portfolio 中放入 Alternative investment
    • 收益增加，风险降低
    • 因为另类投资和传统投资的协方差很小
  • 衡量另类投资
    • 不是正态分布，不能用sharp ratio
    • 用VaR和Sortino ratio
      • Sortino ratio用的semi-variance
    • The use of estimates tends to smooth the return series
  • Tail events
    • 用stress testing，看最坏亏损多少钱

Risk Management

  • An effective risk management process used by alternative investment funds includes segregation of risk and investment process duties

2. Hedge Fund

Characteristics

  • 含有大量derivatives
  • 高杠杆
  • 限制少，不需要年报
  • Restrictions to redemption
    • Lockout period
    • Notice period
  • Hedge fund indices cannot reflect actual performance
    • Survivorship bias
    • Backfill bias
      • 不需要公开数据，业绩不好的不公开

Structure

  • Limited partner (LP)
    • 出资的，有限责任
  • General partner (GP)
    • 管理者，无限责任

Fund of Funds (FOF)

  • 给中小投资者开放门槛，把钱集合起来
  • 优势
    • 给中小投资者提供机会
    • 第一个fund manager可以控制风险
    • 赎回条件更好
    • 可以分散风险
  • 劣势
    • 两份管理费

Hedge Fund Strategies

  • Event-driven strategies
    • 事件发生，赚钱，不发生，亏钱
    • Example
      • Merge Arbitrage：发现A公司想要收购B公司，做空A公司，买入B公司
      • Distressed/Restricting：买入快破产的公司
      • Activist shareholder：买入股票，主动管理公司
  • Relative value strategies
    • 找两个相关性很高的产品，但是涨跌大小有差异，long长得快跌的慢的，short另一个
      • Example
        • 可转债和股票
        • MBS和房价
  • Macro strategies
    • 通过分析宏观经济，找套利机会
  • Equity hedge strategies
    • 防御策略，保证不亏钱
    • Market neutral
      • 构建一个\(\beta=0\)的组合

Hedge Fund Fees

  • Management Fee

    • 年末管理规模 \(\times\) Rate

  • Incentive Fee/Performance Fee

    • 调整后再判断Incentive Fee
      • Management Fee
        • Profit扣除管理费
        • 如果说independently或者before management fee，就不要扣除Management Fee
      • Hurdle rate
        • 只有超出Hurdle rate，才收Incentive fee
        • Hard Hurdle Rate
          • 超出Hurdle Rate的部分收
        • Soft Hurdle Rate
          • 超出后在全部收益基础上收
      • High water mark
        • 每次计算Incentive fee应该跟历史最高点比

  • Notation

    • 2 and 20

      • 2% Management Fee

      • 20% Incentive Fee

  • Example A hedge fund with $98 million of initial capital charges a management fee of 2% and an incentive fee of 20%. The management fee is based on assets under management at year end and the incentive fee is calculated independently from the management fee. The fee structure has a high-water mark provision. The fund value is $112 million at the end of Year 1, ​$100 million at the end of Year, and ​$116 million at the end of Year 3. The net-of-fees return earned by the fund in Year 3 is closest to \[ \begin{array}{cccc} 98&\rightarrow& 112 (-2.24-2.8)&\rightarrow& 106.96\\ 106.96&\rightarrow& 100 (-2)&\rightarrow& 98\\ 98&\rightarrow& 116(-2.32-1.8)&\rightarrow& 111.88\\ \end{array} \] Therefore, return is \[ \frac{111.88-98}{98} = 14.16\% \]

  • Example

    United Capital is a hedge fund with $250 million of initial capital. United charges a 2% management fee based on assets under management at year end, and a 20% incentive fee based on returns in excess of an 8% hurdle rate. In its first year, United appreciates 16%. Assume management fees are calculated using end-of-period valuation. The investor’s net return assuming the performance fee is calculated net of the management fee Hurdle amount = \(250\times 8\% =20\) Management Fee = \(290\times 2\%=5.8\) Incentive Fee = \((290-250-5.8-20)\times 20\%=2.84\) \[ 250\rightarrow 290(-5.8-2.84)\rightarrow 281.36\\ \]

  • Example Ash Lawn Partners, a fund of hedge funds, has the following fee structure

    • 2/20 underlying fund fees with incentive fees calculated independently
    • Ash Lawn fees are calculated net of all underlying fund fees
    • 1% management fee (based on year-end market value)
    • 10% incentive fee calculated net of management fee
    • The fund and all underlying funds have no hurdle rate or high-water mark fee conditions

    In the latest year, Ash Lawn’s fund value increased from $100 million to ​$133 million before deduction of management and incentive fees of the fund or underlying funds. Based on the information provided, the total fee earned by all funds in the aggregate is

    Management Fee1 = \(133\times 1\%=1.33\)

    Incentive Fee1 = \((133-1.33-100)\times 10\%=3.167\)

    Management Fee2 = \((133-1.33-3.167)\times 2\%=2.5701\)

    Incentive Fee2 = \((133-1.33-3.167-100)\times 20\%=5.7\)

    Total = 12.7671

4. Real Estate

Investment Categories

  • Direct
    • 直接买房子或者买地
  • Indirect
    • Real estate investment trusts (REIT)
      • 投资房地产的基金
      • Indirect equity exposure to real estate
    • MBS
      • Indirect debt investment opportunities in real estate

Index

  • Appraisal index
    • 通过对房子估值反应指数变化
    • underestimated volatility
  • Transaction-based (repeat sales) index
    • 只对交易的房子进行估值
    • Sample selection bias

Real Estate Property Valuation

  • Comparable sales approach
    • 找一个有交易的类似房产B
    • Using multiples 在B上进行调整
  • Income approach
    • 类似股票的DCF
    • Property value = Net Operating Income/Capitalization Rate (Discount rate - Growth rate)
    • 但是租金不会连续增长，是阶梯状增长
  • Cost approach
    • 重置房子的成本微调

5. Commodities

Features

  • 投资期货
  • 大宗商品的长期受益比不上股票和债券，用于调整portfolio或抗通胀

How Invest

  • ETF
  • Related common stock
  • Managed futures funds

Spot Price and Future price

  • Future price > Spot price
    • Contango 升水
  • Future price < Spot price
    • Backwardation 贴水
  • 期货的价格由多头和空头决定
  • Future price = Spot price \(\times (1+r_f)\) + Storage costs - Convenience yield
    • Storage costs:
      • 持有期间的成本
    • Convenience yield
      • 持有期间的收益

Sources of return

  • Collateral yield
    • Return earned on margin
    • 保证金有利息
  • Price return
    • 由于现货价格变动导致的收益
  • Roll yield
    • 除掉现货价格波动，剩余的收益
    • 如果市场backwardation，roll yield为正，如果为contango，roll yield为负

Definition of Derivative

  • A contract regarding buying or selling underlying asset in the future
  • 在三个月后以3%利率借100万，算Buy，因为付利息

Why Invest Derivative

  • Hedge
  • Speculate

Four Derivatives

  • Forward Contract

    • Private agreement

  • Future Contract

    • A specialized forward contract
    • Standardized
      • Regulated
    • Guaranteed by clearinghouse
      • Daily Settlement

  • Swap Contract

    • A series of forward contract

  • Option Contract

    • The owner has right but no obligation
    • Option premium
      • 期权费
    • Call option
      • 看涨期权
    • Put option
      • 看跌期权

Classification by Features

  • Forward commitment

    • Forward contract

    • Futures contract

    • Swap contract

  • Contingent claim

    • Option contract

    • CDS

      • 类似于保险合同
      • A买了一个bond，然后向B买了一个CDS，如果bond违约，B要付给A钱

Classification by Trading place

  • Exchange-traded (\(A\rightarrow \text{Clearning House} \rightarrow B\))

    • Futures

    • Option

  • Over-the-counter (OTC) traded (\(A\rightarrow B\))

    • Forward

    • Swap

    • Option

Terminology

  • Forward Commitment

  • Contingent Claim

4. Swaps

Classification

      • Pay-fixed side

Reading 49: Basics of Derivative Pricing and Valuation

0. Risk-Free Arbitrage and No-Arbitrage Rule

Arbitrage

  • 价格低的买入，价格高的卖出

Replication

  • Replicate the payoffs on one asset or portfolio with those of a different asset or portfolio

Pricing

  • Pricing a contract is the process of determining the no-arbitrage price of the underlying asset
  • 标的资产未来买卖的价格
  • Obey no-arbitrage rule

Valuation

  • Valuation of a contract is the process of determining the payoff of the contract during the life of the contract
  • Contract 这张纸现在值多少钱
  • Obey risk neutrality

1. Forwards Pricing and Valuation

Pricing

  • \(FP=(S_0+\theta-\gamma)(1+R_f)^T=S_0(1+R_f)^T-(\gamma - \theta)(1+R_f)^T\)

    • \(\gamma\) is the carrying benefit
      • Dividend, coupon, interest
      • Convenience yield
    • \(\theta\) is the carrying cost
      • Cost of storage
    • \(\gamma-\theta\) denotes the Cost of Carry
    • Zero-coupon bond
    • \(FP=S_0(1+R_f)^T\)

Cash-and-Carry Arbitrage

  • If \(FP>S_0(1+R_f)^T\)

    At Initiation At settlement date
    1. Borrow money \(S_0\) at the risk-free rate \(R_f\)
    2. Buy the underlying bond at the price \(S_0\)
    3. Short the forward contract    		 1. Sell the underlying bound at the price \(FP\)
    2. Repay the loan \(S_0(1+R_f)^T\)
    Profit = \(FP-S_0(1+R_f)^T\)

  • 借钱买现货，现货放身边

Reverse Cash-and-Carry Arbitrage

  • If \(FP<S_0(1+R_f)^T\)

    At Initiation At settlement date
    1. Borrow underlying bond
    2. Sell the underlying bond at the price \(S_0\) and lend the money at risk-free rate \(R_f\)
    3. Long a forward contract    		 1. Buy the underlying bond at the price \(FP\) and repay
    2. Receive investment proceeds
    Profit = \(S_0(1+R_f)^T-FP\)

Valuation

  • T-bill Forwards Valuation

    Time Value
    0 0
    t \(V_{long}=S_t - \frac{FP}{(1+R_f)^{T-t}}\)
    T \(V_{long}=S_T-FP\)

  • Equity Forward Valuation

    Time Value
    0 0
    t \(V_{long}=(S_t-PVD_t) - \frac{FP}{(1+R_f)^{T-t}}\)
    T \(V_{long}=S_T-FP\)

  • Coupon Bonds Forward Valuation

    Time Value
    0 0
    t \(V_{long}=(S_t-PVC_t) - \frac{FP}{(1+R_f)^{T-t}}\)
    T \(V_{long}=S_T-FP\)

2. Futures Pricing and Valuation

Futures vs. Forward

  • If the correlation between the underlying asset value and interest rate is positive. Then prefer futures. Otherwise, prefer forward.
    • 如果股票上升，Futures可以把钱取出来，利率高，收益高
    • 如果股票下降，Futures要补充保证金，利率低，成本低
    • 如果underlying asset是债券，选择Forward

Price

  • 与Forward相同

Value

  • The value of the futures is reset as zero after the contract is marked to market.

3. Forward Rate Agreement (FRA)

Quotation example

  • \(3\times 9\) FRA

  • When to borrow \(\times\) When to repay

  • Borrow and settle in 3 months later. Repay in 9 months later

  • Off-the-run FRA，发行过一段时间的FRA

Underlying asset

  • LIBOR

Settlement and Payoff

  • 第3个月的时候就Settle了

  • Quantitative analysis on payoff

\[ \frac{\text{Principal}\times (\text{floating rate at settlement} - \text{forward rate})\times\frac{\text{days}}{360}}{1+\text{floating rate at settlement}\times\frac{\text{days}}{360}} \]

  • Example

    Long \(1\times 4\) FRA，利率5%，第一个月LIBOR变成4%

    Payoff = \(\frac{(4\%-5\%)\frac{3}{12}\text{Principal}}{1+4\%\times\frac{3}{12}}\)

4. Swap Pricing and Valuation

Pricing of a plain vanilla swap

  • The pricing of swap is the determination of the fixed rate (swap rate).

Valuation

  • Equivalence to bonds

    • An interest rate swap is identical to issuing a fixed-rate bond and using the proceeds to buy a floating-rate bond

  • Equivalence to forward rate agreement (FRA)

    • A swap can be viewed as a series of FRA

    • Difference

      1. FRAs have different forward rate

      2. The next payment is known one period ahead in interest rate swaps. This is not the case for an FRAs.

5. Option Valuation

Moneyness

  • In the money
    • Positive payoff
    • S>X
  • At the money
    • no pay off
    • S=X
  • Out of the money
    • negative payoff
    • S<X

Valuation (Long position)

  • Intrinsic Value

    • Call option
      • \(C=\max[0,S-X]\)
    • Put option
      • \(P=\max[0,X-S]\)

  • Time Value

    • The longer the time, the larger the time value

  • Option value

    • Option value = Intrinsic value + Time value

    • Before expiration

      • Option value > intrinsic value

    • At expiration

      • Option value = intrinsic value

Payoff

  • Long call、Long put、Short call、Short put

Gain/Loss

  • 考虑期权费
  • Long call、Long put 下移
  • Short call、Short put 上移

Factors Affecting the Value of an Option

Sensitivity Factor Calls Puts
Underlying Price Positive related Negative related
Strike Price Negative related Positive related
Payments on the underlying Negative related Positive related
Carrying Cost Positive related Negative related
Volatility Positive related Positive related
Risk-free rate Positive related Negative related
Time to expiration Positive related Positive related*

* When underlying price drops to zero, then negative related

6. Put-Call Parity

  • Definition \[ C+\frac{X}{(1+R_f)^T} = P+S \] where \(C\) is the call option, \(X\) is the strike price, \(S\) is the underlying asset, \(P\) is the put option.

  • Fiduciary Call

    • \(C+\frac{X}{(1+R_f)^T}\) is the fiduciary call.

    • Analysis

      \(S_T<X\) \(S_T>X\)
      Long call payoff 0 \(S_T-X\)
      Long bond payoff \(X\) \(X\)
      Total payoff \(X\) \(S_T\)

  • Protective put

    • \(P+S\) is the protective put

    • Analysis
      | | \(S_T<X\) | \(S_T>X\) | | :---------------: | :-----: | :-----: | | Long put payoff | \(X-S_T\) | \(0\) | | Long stock payoff | \(S_T\) | \(S_T\) | | Total payoff | \(X\) | \(S_T\) |

7. Put-Call-Forward Parity

  • Definition \[ C+\frac{X-FP}{(1+R_f)^T} = P \]

    • \(\frac{X-FP}{(1+R_f)^T}\) 也是一张债券
    • \(FP=S(1+R_f)^T\)

8. Binary Model for Option Valuation

  • \(u=\frac{1}{d}\)
    • \(u\)是上升的比率，\(S_1^+=S_0u\)
    • \(d\)是下降的比率，\(S_1^-=S_0d\)
  • \(\pi_u=\frac{1+R_f-u}{u-d}\)
    • \(\pi_u\) 是上升的概率
    • \(\pi_d=1-\pi_u\) 是下降的概率
  • 折现回当前时间
