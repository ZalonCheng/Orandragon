2. Quantitative methods

Reading 6 Time Value of Money

1. Decomposition of Required Rate of Return

Fisher Effect

  • \((1+R_n)=(1+R_r)(1+I)\)
    • \(R_n \approx R_r+I\)
    • \(R_n\): Nominal interest rate
    • \(R_r\): Real interest rate
    • \(I\): Expected inflation rate

Required Interest Rate on Security

  • \(R_i=R_f+R_p\)
    • \(R_i\): Required rate of return
    • \(R_f\): Nominal risk-free rate
    • \(R_p\): Risk premium
      • Default risk premium: 拖延或者还的少
      • Liquidity risk premium: 流动性差风险高
      • Maturity risk premium: 期限风险，时间长风险越大

2. Effective Annual Rate (EAR)

Calculation

  • \(EAR=\left(1+\frac{r}{m}\right)^m-1\)

Continuous Compounding

  • \(EAR = e^r-1\)

3. Annuities

Calculation

  • \(FV=PV\left(1+\frac{r}{m}\right)^{mn}\)

Requirements

  • Same intervals
  • Same amount of cash
  • Same direction

Classification

  • Ordinary annuities

    • 普通年金，后付年金

  • Annuity due

    • 先付年金

  • Perpetuity

    • 永续年金 \(PV=\frac{A}{r}\)

2. Central Limit Theory

Assumption

  • 样本容量 \(n\) 足够大 (\(n\ge 30\))
  • 总体的 \(\mu\) 和 \(\sigma^2\)已知

Conclusion

  • \(\bar X\sim N(\mu_x,\frac{\sigma^2}{n})\)

Standard Error

  • 样本均值的标准差 \(\sigma_{\bar X}=\frac{\sigma_x}{\sqrt n}\) 叫做 standard error （标准误）
    • 如果总体的variance未知，可以用样本的variance代替

3. Estimation

Properties

  • Unbiasedness
    • $E(X)=$
  • Efficiency
    • 方差小
  • Consistency
    • 当样本容量增加，准确性应该增加
    • 当样本容量增加，standard error应该下降

Estimation Classification

  • Point estimation

    • \(\mu=\bar x\)

  • Confidential interval (CI) estimation 置信区间估计

    • \(\bar X \pm k \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt n}\)

    • \(k\) 对应的百分比叫做 degree of confidence 置信度

    • 剩下的尾巴部分面积叫做level of significance 显著性水平，通常用 \(\alpha\) 表示

Z-Distribution V.S. t-Distribution

  • 方差已知用z
  • 方差未知用t
  • 非正态小样本不可估计
  • 大样本 (\(n\ge 30\)) 任何情况可用z

Reading 11 Hypothesis Testing

1. Critical Value Method

State Hypothesis

  • Null hypothesis (\(H_0\))
    • 想拒绝的假设 (等于号都放在这里边)
  • Alternative hypothesis (\(H_a\))

    • 想接受的假设

    • One tailed and two tailed

      • One tailed
      • \(H_0:\mu=\mu_0\quad H_a:\mu\neq \mu_0\)
      • Two tailed
        • \(H_0: \mu\le \mu_0\quad H_a: \mu>\mu_0\)
        • \(H_0: \mu\ge \mu_0\quad H_a: \mu<\mu_0\)

Test Statistic

  • Calculation

    • \(\frac{\bar X-\mu}{\sigma/\sqrt n}\)

  • Critic Value Selection (就是那个k)

    • 与 \(\alpha\) 有关

    • 与查哪张表有关

    • 与单尾双尾有关

  • Statement

    • Reject the null hypothesis

    • Fail to reject the null hypothesis

2. Hypothesis Testing for Different Parameters

Mean Hypothesis Testing

  • 一个均值
    • z/t
  • 两个均值之间的关系 t分布
    • 独立的
    • 不独立
      • paired comparison test
      • 假设检验差值是否等于0

Variance Hypothesis Testing

  • 一个方差：\(\Chi^2\) 分布
  • 两个方差：F 分布

Correlation Hypothesis Testing

  • Null hypothesis \(H_0: r=0\)

  • \[ t=\frac{r-0}{\sqrt{\frac{1-r^2}{n-2}}}\\ df=n-2 \]

  • r 是相关系数

  • 如果拒绝了原假设，也就是说 \(r\) significantly different from 0，说明有线性关系

3. P-Value Method

Definition

  • 拒绝原假设的最小显著性水平

Compare \(P\) & \(\alpha\)

  • \(P>\alpha\) 不能拒绝原假设

  • P越小越拒绝

4. Type I and Type II error

Definition

  • 原假设正确但是reject了，第一类错误，错杀好人

  • \(P(\text{I error})=\alpha\)

  • 原假设错误但是fail to reject了，第二类错误，放走坏人

Power of test

  • 原假设是错误的，拒绝了

  • \(1-P(\text{II error})\)

Features

  • 当n不变，I error 和 II error此消彼长
  • 当n增大，I和II error都下降

5. Nonparametric test

  • 对非参数检验
    • 当数据不满足分布假设
    • 当数据是排序的
    • 当检验的不是参数

Reading 52: Portfolio Risk and Return (Part I)

1. Return and Risk

Main Theory

  • Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT)
    • Portfolios reduce risk more than they increase returns.

Return Classification

  • Holding Period Return (HPR)

    • \(HPR = \frac{FV-PV}{PV}\)

  • Average Return

    • Arithmetic mean return

    • Geometric mean return

      • Time Weighted Rate of Return (TWRR)
        • \(TWRR=\left(\Pi_{i=1}^N (1+HPR_i)\right)^{1/n}-1\)
          • N: 滚了几期
          • n: 总年数

  • Money-weighted return

    • Money weighted rate of return (MWRR)
      • Annualized Internal Rate of Return (IRR)

  • Relationship between TWRR and MWRR

    • Similarity: Both are annual rates.
    • Difference: TWRR is not influenced by cash flow

  • Example

    • Eric invests $1000 in an account. After one year, the value of the investment is $1200 and Eric adds another $800 into the account. At the end of Year 2, the total value of the investment is $2,200. Calculate the annual TWRR and MWRR.

    • TWRR:14.9%

    • MWRR: 13.62%

  • Other Return Measures

    • Gross return and net return \[ \text{Return}\\ -\text{Transaction Cost (交易费用)}\\ ------------\\ \text{Gross Return}\\ -\text{M&A Cost (基金经理管理费用)}\\ ------------\\ \text{Net Return} \]

    • Pre-tax nominal return and After-tax nominal return:

      • \(\text{After-tax nominal return} = \text{Pre-tax nominal return} \times (1-t)\)

    • Real Return

      • \((1+r_\text{nominal}) = (1+r_\text{real})(1+\pi)\)
      • \(\pi\) is the inflation rate, when \(\pi\) is small, Fisher equation is derived
      • \(r_\text{nominal} = r_\text{real}+\pi\)

    • Leveraged Return

      • Only consider the own money.

Variance of Return for Two Asset Portfolio

  • \(\sigma_{p}^2=\omega_1^2\sigma_1^2+\omega_2^2\sigma_2^2+2\omega_1\omega_2\sigma_1\sigma_2\rho_{1,2}\)

2. Efficient Frontier (EF)

Definition

  • In the case of two asset portfolio, there are three equations

    \[ \begin{array}{rcl} \sigma_{p}^2&=&\omega_1^2\sigma_1^2+\omega_2^2\sigma_2^2+2\omega_1\omega_2\sigma_1\sigma_2\rho_{1,2}\\ E(R)&=&\omega_1R_1+\omega_2R_2\\ \omega_1+\omega_2&=& 1 \end{array} \]

  • The parameters \(R_1,R_2,\sigma_1,\sigma_2,\rho_{1,2}\) are known. If we choose different \(\omega_1\) and \(\omega_2\), we can draw between \(\sigma_{p}\) in terms of x-axis and \(E(R)\) in terms of y-axis.

  • Key Components

    • Opportunity investment set
    • Minimum-variance frontier
    • Global variance minimum point
    • Efficient frontier (EF)
      • Un-efficient 右下方
      • Not achievable 左上方

  • EF Properties

    • Only consider risky assets

    • Given \(E(R)\), \(\sigma\) is minimum

      Given \(\sigma\), \(E(R)\) is maximum

    • Efficient portfolio is well-diversified or fully diversified

Reading 53: Portfolio Risk and Return (Part 2)

1. Capital Market Line (CML)

  • Main Theory: Capital Market Theory (CMT)

  • Capital Market Line (CML)

    • 基于homogeneity of expectation产生CML，是一种特殊的CAL，Optimal risky portfolio in CML叫做market portfolio

    • Risk-free asset 与 market portfolio 的组合产生CML

    • \(E(R_p)=R_f+\frac{R_M-R_f}{\sigma_M}\sigma_p\)

      • Market Sharp Ratio: \(\frac{R_M-R_f}{\sigma_M}\)
      • Portfolio Sharp Ratio: \(\frac{E(R_p)-R_f}{\sigma_p}\)
      • Market Sharp Ratio = Portfolio Sharp Ratio

2. The CAPM and SML

CAPM Core Idea

  • Only systematic risk is compensated.

Nonsystematic Risk and Systematic Risk

  • Nonsystematic risk

    • Can be avoided by forming a diversified portfolio

    • Is local or limited to particular asset.

    • 影响单个公司或者行业

  • Systematic risk (Market risk)

    • A standardized measure of systematic risk is Beta

Estimation of Beta by Security Characteristic Line

  • Security characteristic line (SCL):

    • X: Excess Market Return \(R_m-R_f\)

    • Y: Excess Security Return \(R_i-R_f\)

    • Slope: \(\beta = \frac{\Delta R_i}{\Delta R_m}\) (The sensitivity of security in terms of the change of market)

Compute Beta

  • \(\beta = \frac{\Delta R_i}{\Delta R_m}=\frac{\text{Cov}_{i,m}}{\sigma_m^2}=\rho_{i,m}\frac{\sigma_i}{\sigma_m}\)

  • 市场组合的\(\beta=\frac{\Delta R_m}{\Delta R_m}=1\)

CAPM

  • The security market line (SML) equation

    • \(E(R_i) = R_f+(E(R_m)-R_f)\beta_i\)
      • \(E(R_m)-R_f\): Market risk premium

  • Undervalued, Overestimated and Properly valued.

    • If the point is above the SML, it is undervalued, buy

4. Performance Evaluation Indicators

Sharp Ratio (SR)

  • Definition

    \[ SR=\frac{E(R)-R_f}{\sigma_p} \]

  • Indirect indicator

\(M^2\alpha\) Indicator

  • \(M^2\) definition

    • Create a new portfolio \(P'\) that mimics the risk of a market portfolio with the same sharp ratio as given portfolio and obtain the return as \(M^2=E(R_{P'})=\frac{E(R_p)-R_f}{\sigma_p}\sigma_m+R_f\)

  • \(M^2 \alpha\) definition

    \[ \begin{array}{rcl} M^2 \alpha &=&E(R_{P'})-R_m\\ &=&\frac{E(R)-R_f}{\sigma_p}\sigma_m+R_f-R_m\\ &=&\frac{E(R)-R_f}{\sigma_p}\sigma_m-(R_m-R_f)\\ &=&\Big(\frac{E(R)-R_f}{\sigma_p}-\frac{R_m-R_f}{\sigma_m}\Big)\sigma_m\\ &=&\Big(SR_p-SR_m\Big)\sigma_m\\ \end{array} \]

  • Direct indicator

Treynor Ratio (TR)

  • Definition

    • Only consider systematic risk
    • \(TR=\frac{E(R_p)-R_f}{\beta_p}\)

Jensen's alpha (\(J\alpha\))

  • Alpha 通常用来表示超额收益率

  • \(J\alpha_p = R_p - SML_p =R_p - (R_f+\beta_p(E(R_m)-R_f))\)

  • The difference between real return and the return on the SML

Reading 55: Introduction to Risk Management

1. Define Risk Management

Risk

  • Exposure to uncertainty

Risk Exposure

  • The extent to which an entity’s value may be affected

Risk Management

  • Maximizing utility given a tolerance level of risk

Risk Governance

  • Top-down process and guidance that directs risk management activities to align with and support the overall enterprise

Risk Budgeting

  • Quantifies and allocates the tolerable risk by specific metrics

Procedure

Board Management Establish Management Infrastructure
Goals Strategies Identify Risk
Risk Tolerance Risk Activities Measure Risk
Risk Budgeting Risk Exposure Monitor Risk
Report Risk

    • Credit Risk

    • Liquidity Risk

  • Non-financial Risks

    • Operational Risk
