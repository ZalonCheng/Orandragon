Reading 31 Corporate Governance and ESG

1. Stakeholder Management

Corporate Governance Definition

  • The system of internal controls

  • Corporate governance 保证

    • Shareholders (股东) 利益最大化，或者
    • Stakeholders (公司利益相关者) 利益最大化

Conflict of Interest

  • Shareholders and mangers
    • 长期短期目标冲突
    • 高管high risk averse，股东low risk averse
    • 解决方法：ESOP(股权激励)
  • 大股东小股东
    • 大股东获得控制权
      • A、B股，B股保护大股东
    • Straight voting和cumulative voting
      • Straight voting就是每个人直接投票
      • Cumulative voting可以累积投票，保护小股东
  • 股东和债权人
    • Risk tolerance冲突
    • 债务条款保护债权人

Stakeholder Management Framework

  • Legal Infrastructure
    • Rights established by law
  • Contractual Infrastructure
    • Contractual arrangements between the corporation and stakeholders
    • Corporation has the most control
  • Organizational Infrastructure
    • Internal systems, governance procedures, and practices adopted and controlled
  • Governmental Infrastructure
    • Regulations imposed on companies.
  • 其中2、3可以被公司控制，1、4是强制性的

Reading 32 Capital Budgeting

1. The Basics

Definition

  • Selecting the most profitable long-term projects

Capital Projects Classification

  • Replacement project
    • 把手动设备换成自动设备
  • Expansion project
    • 增加规模，增加新项目
  • Mandatory investment
    • 环保、安全项目
  • Other projects
    • 现金流不太容易预测的项目，比如明星站台
    • 个人喜好产生的项目，比如买私人飞机

Five Basic Principles of Capital Budgeting

  • Based on incremental cash flow
    • 新项目造成的现金流的增加
  • The timing of cash flow is important
  • Cash flow should be analyzed after tax
  • Included
    • Externalities
      • 对外部造成的影响，比如0度可乐
      • Negative externalities
      • Positive externalities
    • Opportunity cost
  • Ignored
    • Sunk cost
      • 与未来现金流无关的，直接扣除，比如咨询费
    • Financing cost
      • 融资成本，考虑在折现率内

2. Projects Evaluation Methods

  • Net Present Value (NPV)

    • Definition

      • \(NPV=\displaystyle \sum_{t=0}^n\frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t}\)

    • 缺点

      • 没有考虑初始规模

    • Example

      Firm’s Cost of Capital = 9%

      Year 0 1 2 3
      Net Cash Flow -100 25 50 75

      NPV=22.93

  • Internal Rate of Return (IRR)

    • Definition

      • 单位投入的回报率

      • NPV=0时的折现率

    • 缺点

      • 可能产生无解或者多解
        • CF符号变动几次，最多出现几个IRR
        • 变动一次的叫做conventional cash flow
        • 变动多次的叫做unconventional cash flow

    • 如果NPV与IRR冲突，优先选用NPV

    • Example

      Year 0 1 2 3
      Net Cash Flow -100 25 50 75

      IRR = 19.44%

  • Payback period (PBP)

    • 收回投资成本需要多久

    • 缺点：没有考虑回本后的现金流

    • Example

      Year 0 1 2 3
      Net Cash Flow -100 25 50 75
      Cumulative Cash Flow -100 -75 -25 50

      在第二年和第三年之间回本，PBP = 2+25/75 = 2.33

  • Discounted PBP (DPBP)

    • 所有现金流折现算回收期

    • 缺点：没有考虑回本后的现金流

    • Example

      Year 0 1 2 3
      Net Cash Flow -100 25 50 75
      Discounted Net Cash Flow -100 22.94 42.08 57.91
      Cumulative Net Cash Flow -100 -77.06 -34.98 22.93

      DBP = 2 + 34.98/57.91 = 2.604

  • Profitable index (PI)

    • Definition \[ PI=\frac{\text{PV of future cash flow}}{|\text{CF}_0|}=1+\frac{NPV}{|\text{CF}_0|} \]

    • 缺点

      • 没有考虑绝对回报金额

    • Example

      Firm’s Cost of Capital = 9%

      Year 0 1 2 3
      Net Cash Flow -100 25 50 75

      PI = 1 + 22.93/100 = 1.2293

3. NPV Profiles

  • Plot

    • 横轴是Cost of Capital (Discount Rate)
      • 与横轴交点是IRR
    • 纵轴是NPV
      • 与纵轴交点是现金流直接加总

  • Crossover Rate

    • 两个项目NPV相等时的IRR

  • NPV和IRR冲突的原因

    • 大额现金流出现时机，大额现金流出现越晚，随r变动越快

4. Impact of NPV Rule and Stock Price

  • In theory

    • 股票价格波动量 \(\Delta P=\frac{NPV}{\text{Stock\# }}\)

  • In reality

    • 预计的NPV已经反映在股票价格上了，如果实际NPV低于预期NPV，价格会下降

Reading 33 Cost of Capital

1. Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC)

  • Structure

    • Debt

    • Equity

      • Preferred stock
      • Common stock

    • Calculation

      Cost of Capital Weight
      Debt \(r_d(1-t)\) \(w_d\)
      Preferred stock \(r_{ps}\) \(w_{ps}\)
      Common stock \(r_s\) \(w_s\)

      \[ WACC=w_dr_d(1-t) + w_{ps}r_{ps}+w_sr_s \]

    • \(t\) is marginal tax rate，借债的利息有tax shield作用

2. How to Find Weights

  • 计算权重选用 market value

  • Capital Structure Definition

    = Debt/Equity

    \(w_d=D/(D+E)\)

    \(w_s=E/(D+E)\)

  • How to choose market structure

    • Target capital structure
    • Trends in capital structure
    • Current capital structure
    • Average of comparable company’s structure

  • Example

    Fran is estimating the cost of capital of Frontier Corp. She has gathered the following information.

    Current Year Forecasted for the Next Year
    Book Value of Debt 50 50
    Market Value of Debt 62 63
    Book Value of Equity 55 58
    Market Value of Equity 210 220

    \[ w_d=\frac{63}{63+220}=0.223\\ w_e=1-w_d=0.777 \]

3. How to Find Cost of Capital

Cost of Debt \(r_d\)

  • YTM Approach

  • 已知PV

    计算区间利率

    年化得到 \(r_d\)

    Cost of debt = \(r_d(1-t)\)

  • Debt-Rating Approach

    新公司不知道债券PV，找信用评级类似的企业

  • Example

    Smith Inc. bond with remaining 5 Years is sold at 1030, Par value is 1000. Coupon rate is 10%. Coupon is paid semiannually. The marginal tax rate is 30%. N=10. PV=1030. PMT=-50. FV=-1000. 求得 I/Y = 4.618 YTM = \(4.618\times 2=9.237%\) Cost of debt = \(9.237(1-30\%)=6.47\)

Cost of Preferred Stock \(r_{ps}\)

  • Perpetual annuity

    \[ r_{ps}=\frac{D_1}{P_0} \] \(D_1\): Preferred dividends

    \(P_0\): Market price of the preferred stock

    Convertible preferred stock: risk reduce, price increase, \(r_{ps}\) reduce

    Callable preferred stock: risk increase, price increase, \(r_{ps}\) increase

Cost of Equity (Common Stock) \(r_s\)

  • CAPM approach \[ r_e = R_f + \beta (R_m-R_f) \]

    • \(R_f\): 长期国债收益率

    • \(\beta=\frac{\text{Cov}_{i,m}}{\sigma^2_m}\)

    • 上市公司\(\beta\)已知，直接求

    • 非上市公司使用pure-play method

      求A公司\(\beta\)，找已上市的comparable B公司

      Risk分为Business risk和Financial risk

      B公司去杠杆得到 \(\beta_{asset}=\beta_B\frac{E}{E+D(1-t)}\)

      \(\beta_{asset}\)加杠杆得到 \(\beta_A=\beta_{asset}\frac{E'+D'(1-t')}{E'}\)

      再用CAPM计算\(r_s\)

    • 考虑Country Risk Premium (CRP)

      • Sovereign yield spread (SYS)

        国家信用的差值，用长期国债利率表示，要转换成股票市场 \[ CRP=\frac{SYS}{\sigma_{bond}}\sigma_{stock} \]

      • Cost of capital \[ r_e=R_f +\beta[R_m-R_f+CRP] \]

    • Example

      Current market price per share $28
      Most recent dividend per share paid (\(D_0\)) $2
      Expected dividend payout rate 40%
      Expected return on equity (ROE) 15%
      Beta for the common stock 1.3
      Expected rate of return on the market portfolio 13%
      Risk-free rate of return 4%

      \(r_e=r_f+\beta[r_M-r_f]=4+1.3[13-4]=15.7\)

  • Dividend Discount Model (DDM) approach

    • Golden growth model (GGM)

      假设所有股利按照增长率\(g\)增长 \[ P_0=\frac{D_1}{r_e-g}\\ r_e=\frac{D_1}{P_0}+g \] \(D_1\): 第一期的dividend

      \(D_1/P_0\): dividend yield

      \(g\): Sustainable growth rate

      \(g=\text{retention rate}\times ROE\)

    • Example | Current market price per share | $30 | | ------------------------------------------- | :---: | | Most recent dividend per share paid (\(D_0\)) | $2.5 | | Expected dividend payout rate | 50% | | Expected return on equity (ROE) | 15% | \(g=0.5\times 0.15=0.075\) \(r_e=\frac{D_1}{P_0}+g=\frac{2.5(1+g)}{30}+g=16.5\)

  • Bond yield plus risk premium approach \[ r_e=\text{bond yield} + \text{risk premium} \]

    • Bond risk: market yield of firm long-term bond
    • Risk premium: Historical spread between bond yield and stock

4. Optimal Capital Budget

  • Overestimated and underestimated

    If a project risk > firms’ risk，如果用公司的WACC计算，NPV is overestimated.

  • Break point

    WACC改变总资本的临界点

  • Optimal Capital Budget Figure

    • 融资成本不断上升，边际收益不断下降，因为好的项目越来越少

5. Floatation Cost

  • Method 1

    使用GGM公式 \[ r_e'=\frac{D_1}{P_0-F}+g \] \(F\): 发行成本

    或者 \[ r_e' = \frac{D_1}{P_0(1-f)}+g \] \(f\): 发行费用所占比率

  • Method 2

    发行成本作为\(CF_0\)直接流出，更符合实际情况

Reading 34 Measures of Leverage

1. Definition

  • Operating leverage results from fix operating cost

  • Financial leverage results from the use of debt

2. Leverage

  • Degree of Operating Leverage (DOL) \[ \begin{array}{rcl} DOL&=&\frac{\Delta EBIT/EBIT}{\Delta Q/Q}\\ EBIT&=& \text{Sales} - TVC-FC\\ &=& (P-VC)Q-FC\\ \Delta EBIT&=& (P-VC)\Delta Q\\ DOL&=& (P-VC)Q/EBIT\\ &=& \frac{\text{Sales}-TVC}{\text{Sales}-TVC-FC} \end{array} \]

    • 杠杆对经营利润的放大效应

  • Degree of financial leverage (DFL) \[ \begin{array}{rcl} DFL&=&\frac{\Delta EPS/EPS}{\Delta EBIT/EBIT}\\ EPS&=& \frac{NI}{\text{stock # }}=\frac{EBIT-I}{\text{stock # }} \text{（不考虑税）}\\ \Delta EPS&=& \frac{\Delta NI}{\text{stock # }}=\frac{\Delta EBIT}{\text{stock # }}\\ DFL&=& \frac{EBIT}{EBIT-I}\\ \end{array} \]

  • Degree of total leverage (DTL) \[ \begin{array}{rcl} DTL&=&\frac{\Delta EPS/EPS}{\Delta Q/Q}\\ &=& DOL \times DFL\\ &=& \frac{\text{Sales}-TVC}{\text{Sales}-TVC-FC-I} \end{array} \]

3. Breakeven Quantity

  • Breakeven quantity of sales \(Q_{BE}\)

    Consider fixed financial cost and fixed operating cost

    Revenue \(=\) Cost

    FC是经营性固定成本

    I是财务性固定成本 \[ \begin{array}{rcl} PQ&=&FC+I+VC\times Q\\ Q_{BE}&=& \dfrac{FC+I}{P-VC} \end{array} \] P-VC: Marginal contribution

  • Operating breakeven quantity of sales \(Q_{OBE}\)

    Only consider fixed operating cost \[ \begin{array}{rcl} Q_{OBE}&=& \dfrac{FC}{P-VC} \end{array} \]

  • 杠杆越好，FC和I越高，Q越高

Reading 25 Inventory Recognition

1. Inventory Recognition

Definition

  • 企业在日常活动中持有以备出售的在产品、产成品和原材料

Product Costs (Capitalizing)

  • 卖出的时候进入利润表

  • 在达到可供出售状态前的一切必要支出

  • 外购

    • 购买价
    • 相关税费
    • 其他（运输、原材料存储）

  • 加工

    • 料：原材料
    • 工：工人工资
    • 费：制造费用，间接费用，比如：仓库管理费用

Period Expense (Expensing)

  • 直接进入利润表

  • 非正常损耗

  • 生产完后的存储费用

  • 行政管理费用

2. Valuation Methods

  • 确定inventory价格

  • IFRS

    • Specific identification
    • FIFO
    • Weighted average cost

  • GAAP

    • IFRS
    • LIFO

3. Analysis of Valuation Method

  • LIFO: I/S accurate

    FIFO: B/S accurate

  • In the periods of rising prices

    Analyze LIFO

    • I/S
      • Higher COSG
      • Lower EBIT
      • Lower Tax
      • Lower Net income
    • B/S
      • Lower Inventory
      • Lower working capital
    • C/F
      • Higher CFO (because lower tax)

4. Inventory System

  • 确定库存数量

  • Periodic inventory system

    • 在accounting period最后盘点存货，不能实时知道仓库存货
    • 数量x价格=Ending Inventory
    • Beginning Inventory + Purchase - COGS = Ending Inventory
      • 可以求出COGS

  • Perpetual inventory system

    • 每次卖出存货都盘点
    • 数量1x价格1 = COGS1
    • 数量2x价格2 = COGS2
    • Beginning Inventory + Purchase - (COGS1+COGS2+…) = Ending Inventory
      • 可以求出Ending Inventory

  • 采用FIFO，Periodic和Perpetual结果是相同的

  • 采用LIFO，Periodic结果可能错误（寅吃卯粮）

  • Valuation methods 和 Inventory System 共同决定了记账方式

5. Impairment of Inventory

  • 存货是历史成本计量

  • IFRS

    • 出现减值信号或至少一年一次
    • 做减值测试
      • 可变现净值 Net realizable value (NRV)
      • NRV = Selling price（预期售价）- Selling cost（达到预期售价的必要成本）
    • 减值（BV 账面价值>NRV）
      • Inventory is write down to NRV on B/S
      • A loss is recognized in I/S
        • 减值有可能直接计在COGS里
        • 但最好放在impairments里，在infrequent and unusual items里
      • 减值的本质是把未来的销货成本提前
    • 减值迹象消失，可以冲回去，但不能超过历史价格

  • GAAP

    • 比较BV和Market

    • $$ =

      \[\begin{array}{|rcl} &-&\text{NRV}\\ &-&\\ &-& \text{Replacement cost}\\ &-&\\ &-& \text{NRV}-\text{Normal profit margin} \end{array}\]

      $$

      Market取Replacement cost或者上限NRV或者下限NRV-Normal profit margin

      NRV-Normal profit margin本质是不要利润，平价卖出

    • 如果BV>Market，进行减值，跟IFRS一样

    • 减值不能恢复

  • Inventory Adjustments

    • Agricultural, forest products, minerals may be measured at Net realizable value (NRV).
      • 在这里NRV = Fair value - Selling cost，而不是Selling price
    • 直接记在I/S的gain or loss里，不计减值

6. LIFO Reserve

  • GAAP要求，如果用LIFO，必须在Footnotes披露difference between LIFO and FIFO，就是LIFO Reserve
  • B/S: LIFO Inv. + LIFO Res. = FIFO Inv.
    • I/S: LIFO COGS - \(\Delta\)LIFO Res. = FIFO COGS
  • Changes in accounting policy
    • 其他方法改LIFO: Prospective application
    • 其他改动：Retrospective application
  • LIFO改成FIFO的报表调整
    • 期初
      • Inventory +Res1
      • RE +Res1(1-t)
      • Cash/Tax payable -/+Res1 x t
    • 期末
      • Inventory +Res2
      • RE +Res2(1-t)
      • Cash/Tax payable -/+Res2 x t

7. LIFO Liquidation

  • 正常情况
    • 物价上升，存货数量上升，LIFO Reserve一定在上升
  • 不正常情况
    • 物价下降或者存货数量下降都会导致LIFO Reserve下降
    • 物价下降导致的LIFO Reserve下降还是可以接受的
    • 当存货数量下降导致的LIFO Reserve下降，叫做LIFO Liquidation
      • 所以LIFO Liquidation一定会导致LIFO Reserve下降
      • 但是LIFO Reserve下降不一定是LIFO Liquidation
      • 如果发生LIFO Liquidation，会导致COGS降低，会导致税增加，净利润增加（分析师应该调整报表），但这不是可持续的

Reading 26 Long-Lived Assets

1. Capitalizing and Expensing

  • Definition
    • Capitalizing
      • Cash变成别的Asset
      • The asset capitalized today will be expensed in the future
    • Expensing
      • Cash直接进入到I/S
  • Impact on C/F（只针对long-lived asset）
    • Capitalized expenditures are classified as CFI
    • Expensed expenditures are classified as CFO
  • Capitalizing V.S. Expensing
    • Capitalizing
      • NI 先高后低
      • Asset一直高
      • Equity一直高
      • ROA和ROE先高后低
      • Net income volatility 低，因为资本化后钱逐渐进入I/S
      • CFO 高
      • CFI 低

2. Tangible Asset (PPE)

Capitalized Parts

  • 固定资产达到可使用状态之前的一切必要支出为Capitalizing

  • \[ \begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|c|}\hline \text{Purchase}&----&\text{Ready for use}&----------&\text{End of life}\\\hline &\begin{array}{c} \text{买来必要的费用}\\ \textbf{Capitalize} \end{array}&& \begin{array}{c} \text{Depreciation}\\ \text{Repair 小修小补}\\ \text{Staff training}\\ \textbf{Expense}\\\hline \text{Enhencement}\\ \textbf{Capitalize} \end{array}\\\hline \end{array} \]

Capitalizing Interest Costs

  • 如果借的钱买了PPE，则利息必须跟着设备一起资本化进入B/S，这部分利息随着折旧和摊销进入I/S，并且应出现在EBIT之前

  • 作为CFA在计算Interest coverage ratio时应该将这部分利息拿回I/S中的Interest里

  • 如果借的钱没有一次性花完，剩下的钱做短期理财，则利息应该记为净利息

  • 在C/F中，资本化的利息费用作为CFI流出，费用化的利息在GAAP中记为CFO，在IFRS中记为CFO或CFF

  • Capitalized Interest V.S. Expensed Interest

    • Capitalized Interest
      • NI 先高后低
      • EBIT 低
      • Interest expense 先低后相同
      • Interest coverage ratio 先高后低
      • CFI 先低后一样
      • CFO 先高后一样

  • Example

    • Total Asset = 2000

    Capitalized interest costs = 50

    EBIT = 900

    Interest expense in I/S = 150

    Tax rate = 10%

    Depreciation expense due to the capitalized interest = 10

    CFO = 450

    CFI = -250

    • As a CFA, should do the following adjustments

    Interest = \(150+50 = 200\)

    EBIT = \(900 + 10 = 910\)

Reading 27 Income Taxes

1. Tax Reporting & Financial Reporting

Difference Between Two Systems

Income Statement Tax Return （纳税申报表）
Revenue 编写完Income Statement \(\rightarrow\) Taxable Revenue
-Expense -Deductible Expenses
EBT (Accounting Profit) \(\rightarrow\) 开始编写Tax Return Taxable Income
-Income tax expense Current tax payable (=Taxable Income \(\times\) Tax rate) \(\rightarrow\) 返回 Income Statement
Net Income
(Accrual Based) (Cash Based)

Sources of Difference

  • Temporary difference

    例如：Unrealized gain or loss, depreciation

    会产生DTA和DTL

    DTL会导致企业未来多缴税

    DTA会导致企业未来少缴税

    DTA和DTL不允许netted

  • Permanent difference

    例如：国债的利息收益，行政罚款等

  • Income tax expense = Current taxes payable + \(\Delta\)DTL - \(\Delta\)DTA

    • Current taxes payable: Cash tax
    • \(\Delta\)DTL - \(\Delta\)DTA: Deferred tax

Calculation

  • 计算Taxable income

  • 计算Current tax payable = Taxable income \(\times\) 当前税率t

  • 计算Temporary difference

  • 计算DTL和DTA = Temporary difference \(\times\) 未来预期税率t

  • 计算\(\Delta\)DTL和\(\Delta\)DTA

  • 计算Income tax expense = Current tax payable + \(\Delta\)DTL - \(\Delta\)DTA

2. Effective Tax Rate & Statutory Tax Rate

  • Taxes payable = Taxable income \(\times\) Tax rate

    其中Tax rate是Statutory tax rate

  • Tax Expense = EBT (Accounting Profit) \(\times\) Tax rate

    其中Tax rate是Effective tax rate

  • 如果不存在Permanent difference

    则Effective tax rate = Statutory tax rate

    如果存在Permanent difference，要进行disclose，要disclose为什么有差异

  • Statutory tax rate是给出的，Effective tax rate是自己算出的

3. Tax Base & Carrying Value

Definition

  • 只有暂时性差异是Difference between tax base & carrying value

  • 可以直接比较Tax base和Carrying value判断DTA和DTL

  • 通过I/S和B/S都可以判断DTA和DTL，但建议使用B/S

  • Tax base 就是税务机关眼中资产和负债值多少钱

Tax Base of Asset

  • 可以在将来纳税申报表中可抵扣的金额就是资产现在的tax base

Tax Base of Liability

  • 负债的账面价值减去负债在将来纳税申报表中可抵扣的金额

  • (考试中基本为0)

Tax Base and Accounting Base of Assets

  • Depreciable assets

    • Accounting base

      = Original cost - Accumulated accounting depreciation - Impairment

    • Tax base

      = Original cost - Accumulated tax depreciation

  • Research in R&D

    • Accounting base (Expense)

      = Zero

    • Tax base (Capitalized) 担心少缴税，所以资产化

      = Original cost - Accumulated amortization

  • Accounting receivable

    • Accounting base

      = Invoiced amount - allowance for bed debt

    • Tax base

      = Invoiced amount

Tax Base and Accounting Base of Liabilities

  • Customer advance (Unearned revenue)

  • Accounting base

    Treat as liability

  • Tax base

    今天就要交税，不确认负债

  • Warranty liability

    • Accounting base

      Treat as liability

    • Tax base

      Recognized when cash flow is incurred

Determine DTA/DTL

Items Carrying Value > Tax Base Carrying Value < Tax Base
Assets DTL DTA
Liability DTA DTL

  • 资产账面价值大于计税基础产生DTL

  • 其中数值是difference\(\times\)未来预计税率

Tax Loss Carrying Forward

  • Tax loss carrying forward also results in a DTA

    之前的亏损可以在今天盈利的基础上减去，这就是Tax loss carrying forward

4. Calculation of Deferred Tax Items & Income Tax Expense

  • I/S approach

    比较EBT和Taxable income的temporary difference，得到\(\Delta\)DTA和\(\Delta\)DTL

    计算DTA和DTL

    计算Income tax expense = Taxes payable + \(\Delta\)DTL - \(\Delta\)DTA

  • B/S approach

    比较账面价值和计税基础得到DTA和DTL

    计算\(\Delta\)DTA和\(\Delta\)DTL

    计算Income tax expense

  • 如果利率发生变化，两者结果不同，B/S approach是正确的

  • 建议使用B/S approach

  • Example

    Accounting Dep. (2) (2) (2)
    Tax Dep. (3) (2) (1)
    \(\Delta\)DTL 0.3 0 -0.3
    DTL 0.3 0.3 0
    Carrying base 4 2 0
    Tax base 3 1 0
    DTL 0.3 0.3 0
    \(\Delta\)DTL 0.3 0 -0.3

5. Changes in Income Tax Rates

  • Income Tax Expense = Taxes payable + \(\Delta\)DTL - \(\Delta\)DTA

    • 计算Taxes payable用Current tax rate
    • 计算\(\Delta\)DTL和\(\Delta\)DTA用future tax rate

  • \((\text{DTL or DTA})_\text{ending}\) = Temporary difference \(\times\) New tax rate

    所以即使Temporary difference不变，税率变化也会影响\(\Delta\)DTL和\(\Delta\)DTA

  • 税率变化的影响

    未来税率变化不一定怎么影响Income tax expense

    当前税率的升高会增加Income tax expense

6. Definition and Classification of Leases

Two parties

  • Lessee: use the asset
  • Lessor: owner of the asset

Two types

  • Operating lease

    经营租赁，所有权不变更

  • Finance lease

    租赁期限长，资产没必要转回

    实质：借钱买资产

    所有权发生变更，进入Lessee的B/S的使用权资产

Lessee Structure

  • IFRS: Finance lease
  • GAAP: Finance lease and operating lease* (特殊的operating lease，资产也要入账)
  • Exceptions
    • 短期租赁行为，IFRS和GAAP都可以使用operating lease，
    • 低价值租赁，IFRS可以使用operating lease

Lessor Structure

  • IFRS: Finance lease and operating lease

  • GAAP: Finance lease (sales-type, direct finance) and operating lease

  • 判断：

    • 是否满足FL和OL区分的一般条件
    • 是否很有可能收到租赁费
    • Yes (都满足): 作为GAAP Sales-type finance lease (IFRS Finance lease)
    • No (有一条不满足): 是否有第三方担保或出租人通过收取承租人租赁费的方式换回成本
      • Yes (满足其中一个): GAAP Direct finance lease
      • No (都不满足): GAAP Operating lease (IFRS Operating lease)

9. Brief Introduction of Pension Plans

  • Defined contribution plans (DC plan)

    • 设定提存计划
    • 充钱固定
    • 可以转移到其他公司，员工自负盈亏

  • Defined benefit plan (DB plan)

    • 设定收益计划
    • 员工拿钱固定
    • 负债，公司管钱，员工不负盈亏

  • Brief introduction of DB accounting

    • PBO: Projected benefit obligation

      PBO = Final wage \(\times\) N (工作年数) $$ 1%

    • Fund Status

      Fund Status = Fair value of plan assets (PA) - PBO

      PA和PBO在B/S上要相互抵消

      Fund Status>0: Overfunded，B/S上只有Net Plan Assets

      Fund Status<0: Underfunded，B/S上只有Net Plan Liability
