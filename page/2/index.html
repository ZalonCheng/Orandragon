    • Obligation，提前偿还本金，类似于Amortization

        • Higher reinvestment risk

Coupon Payment Structure

  • Floating-Rate Notes (FRN)

    • Coupon rate = Reference rate ± Quoted margin

      • 通常是加号

      • Quoted margin 单位是 bps

        1 bps = 0.01%

      • Reference rate通常是LIBOR

        LIBOR是单利计算，360天计算

    • Example

      CR = LIBOR + 50 bps

    • Reference rate determination

      • Reference rate 用期初决定期末利率
      • Reference rate 要match付息频率

    • Cap and Floor

      • The upper limit is called cap 有利于issuer
      • The lower limit is called floor 有利于investor
      • Cap and floor都有叫做Collar

    • 如果Quoted margin也是浮动的

      • Variable-Rate Note

  • Inverse Floater

    • 与Reference rate变化相反

    • CR=5%\(-\)系数\(\times\)LIBOR

      • 如果系数>1，叫做Leveraged Inverse Floater

      • 如果系数<1，叫做Deleveraged Inverse Floater

      • 5% is the maximum coupon rate

    • Example

      An inverse floater’s coupon rate = 15%\(-2\times \text{(3-month LIBOR)}\). Suppose the 3-month LIBOR is 3%. Then the coupon rate is 9%.

  • Others

    • Step-up Coupon Rate

      • Coupon 越来越大

      • 一般都带Call option

    • Deferred Coupon Bond

      • 延迟支付Coupon

      • Zero-Coupon Bond

    • Credit-Linked Coupon Bond

      • 信用评级下降，Coupon增加

    • Pay-in-Kind (PIK) Bond

      • 把债券当做利息支付，但是本金用现金偿还

    • Index-Linked Bond

      • 跟某指数挂钩

      • Inflation-linked bonds 抗通胀债券

      • Treasury inflation protected security (TIPS) 美国抗通胀国债

        • Coupon Rate不变
        • Principle变
        • Coupon变
        • 本金保护债券，本金调整不能低于最初

4. Bonds with Contingency Provisions

Callable bond

  • 给发行人权利，当r下降，以Call price进行Call back，重新借债，有利于issuer
  • \(V_\text{call bond} = V_\text{bond} - V_\text{call}\)
  • Deferred call
    • Call provision 有一段延迟期
  • Call price
    • 行权价格
  • Call premium
    • 超过par value的那部分call price
  • First par call date
    • 第一个可以以面值call back的日期
  • The style of exercise for callable bonds
    • American style: 随时
    • European style: 某一天
    • Bermuda style: 某一天后的特定时间
  • Make-whole call provision
    • Call Price 定为未来现金流的折现求和

Puttable bond

  • 给bondholder权利，当利率上涨，以put price进行put back，重新投资
  • 有利于bondholder
  • \(V_\text{put bond}=V_\text{bond} + V_\text{put}\)

Convertible bond

  • 给bondholder的权利，可以转换成Common stock

  • Conversion price

    • 行权价格

  • Conversion ratio

    • 一份bond转多少股

  • Conversion value

    • 一份bond转换后持有股票的总市值
      • Market price of the stock \(\times\) Conversion ratio

  • Conversion parity

    • At parity: Conversion value 等于 Convertible bond’s price

    • Above parity: Conversion value 大于 Convertible bond’s price，行权

    • Below parity: Conversion value 小于 Convertible bond’s price，不行权

  • Two advantage to issuer

    • Reduce interest expense
    • Reduce debt

Warrants

  • Beneficial to the bondholders
  • Attached option but not embedded option
  • 超过某行权价格，可以以某一价格购买公司股票

Contingent Convertible bonds (CoCos)

  • 一旦达到约定，bond自动转为股票

5. Repurchase Agreement (Repo Agreement)

  • Definition

    \[ \text{A}\quad \begin{array}{c} \displaystyle\operatorname*\longrightarrow^\text{bond}\\ \displaystyle\operatorname*\longleftarrow_\text{Selling Price} \end{array}\quad \text{B}\\ \text{A}\quad \begin{array}{c} \displaystyle\operatorname*\longleftarrow^\text{bond}\\ \displaystyle\operatorname*\longrightarrow_\text{Repurchase Price} \end{array}\quad \text{B}\\ \]

  • Repurchase rate

    • Lender的收益率
      • \(\text{Repo Rate}= \frac{\text{Repurchase Price}-\text{Selling Price}}{\text{Selling Price}}\)
    • 风险高repo rate高

  • Repo Margin

    • 对抵押品打的折扣
    • Repo margin (Haircut) = Market Value of Collateral - Selling price

  • 站在borrower角度看，叫做Repo agreement，站在lender角度，叫做Reverse repo agreement

  • Repo agreement is not regulated

    • Credit risk
      • 抵押品下跌，lender面临credit risk
      • 抵押品上升，borrower面临credit risk

Reading 44 Introduction to Fixed Income Valuation

1. Bond Valuation Process

  • 预期现金流，决定折现率，未来现金流折现求和

Yield to Maturity (YTM)

  • 持有到期收益率

  • Assumption

    • Hold the bond until maturity
    • No default
    • Coupon are reinvested at original YTM

  • 是债券的IRR

  • Example 1 An investor buys a 25-year 10 percent annual pay bond for $900, planning to sell in 5 years when he estimates yield will be 9 percent. What is the estimate of the future price of this bond. N = 20. I/Y= 9. PMT = 100. FV=1000. 得到 PV = 1091

  • Example 2 An Analyst observes a 5-year 10% semiannual pay bound. The face amount is 1000. The analyst believes that the YTM on a semiannual bound basis should be 15%. Based on this yield estimate, the price of this bound would be N = 10. I/Y = 7.5% PMT=50. FV=1000. 得到PV = 828

Relation between Price and Yield

  • 对于option-free bond
  • 类似于分数函数 \(P=\frac{1}{YTM}\)，是convex的
  • YTM左右相同大小，Price涨多跌少，对投资者有利

Relation between Price and Time

  • 与Amortization的图相同，趋向于Par value

2. Spot Rate and Accrued Interest

Spot Rate

  • 起始点是0时刻，看未来n年的利率

  • 期间只有一笔现金流

  • \(SR_n=\) n年期0息债券的YTM

Forward Rate

  • 起点是t时刻

No-Arbitrage Price

  • 用Spot rate计算

    • \(\frac{CP_1}{1+S_1}+\frac{CP_2}{(1+S_2)^2}+\dotsm+\frac{CP_N+Par}{(1+S_N)^N}\)

Full Price, Clean Price, Accrued Interest

  • 应该给到卖方A的Coupon叫做Accrued interest

    • Accrued interest = coupon \(\times \frac{t}{T}\)
      • t是Settlement date 到前一次Coupon date 的时间
      • T是Coupon之间间隔的时间

  • Full price (Dirty price) = Clean price (Quoted price, Flat price) + Accrued interest

    • 买方真正支付给卖方的价格

  • 计算Full price

    • 可以计算到前一个Coupon date的Full price (此时Accrued Interest是0)
    • 然后滚到Settling date，最后减去Accrued interest可以得到Clean price

  • Example A 6% German corporate bond is priced for settlement on 18 June 2015. The bond market makes semiannual coupon payments on Mar. 19 and 19 Sep. of each year and matures on 19 Sep. 2026. The corporate bond uses the 30/360 day-count convention for accrued interest. YTM=6%. Principal = 100. First calculate to the data Mar. 19 2015. Full price = 100. Convert to June 18, 2015. Full price = \(100(1+0.03)^{\frac{89}{180}}=101.47\) Accrued Interest = \(3\times \frac{89}{180}=1.48\) Clean price = \(101.47-1.48=99.99\)

4. Yield Measure

Effective Annual Rate

  • Definition

    • \(EAR = \left(1+\frac{YTM_m}{m}\right)^{m}-1\)

  • Convert YTM from \(m\) periods per year to \(n\) periods per year

    • \(\left(1+\frac{YTM_m}{m}\right)^{m}=\left(1+\frac{YTM_n}{n}\right)^{n}\)
      • YTM = Annual percentage rate (APR)

Street Convention Yield

  • Neglect weekend and holidays

True Yield

  • Consider all days.

    Street yield \(\ge\) True yield

    Because the weekends and holidays delay the time to payment in True yield.

    现金流向后移动，需要更小的YTM来补偿

Current Yield

  • Current yield = \(\frac{\text{Sum of Coupon in a Year}}{\text{Flat Bond Price}}\)

Coupon Rate, Current Yield, YTM Relationship

  • Par bond

    Coupon rate = Current yield = Yield to Maturity

  • Discount bond

    Coupon rate < Current yield < Yield to Maturity

  • Premium bond

    Coupon rate > Current yield > Yield to Maturity

Yield to Call (Put)

  • 假设行权情况下的Yield

  • YTM和Yield to Call两者最小的叫做 Yield to Worst

  • Example An analyst observes a Co. 7.125% 4-year, semiannual-pay bond trading at 1023.47. The bond is callable at 1010 in two year. What is the bond’s yield-to-call? N = 4. PV = 1023.47. PMT = 71.25/2. FV = 1010. I/Y = 3.1670. Yield-to-call = 3.1670*2 = 6.334.

Option-Adjusted Price and Option-Adjusted Yield (OAY)

  • 不考虑Option的Price和Yield

Yield Measures for Floating Rate Notes

  • Coupon rate = Reference rate + Quoted margin

  • Discount rate (YTM) = Reference rate + Required margin (Discount margin)

  • A floating-rate note has a quoted margin of +50 basis points and a required margin of +75 basis points. On its next reset date, the price of the note will be less than the par value.

Yields for Money Market Instruments

  • Discount yield (U.S. Treasury, Commercial paper)
    • DR=\(\frac{\text{Days in a Year}}{\text{days}}\left(\frac{FV-PV}{FV} \right)\)
  • Add-on yield (LIBOR)
    • AOR=\(\frac{\text{Days in a Year}}{\text{days}}\left(\frac{FV-PV}{PV} \right)\)
  • Bond equivalent yield for money market security
    • 365-day add-on rate basis

6. Yield Spread

  • 债券的风险补偿

  • Structure \[ \begin{array}{ccccl} \text{Spread} &=& \text{Risk premium} & \left\{\begin{array}{l} \text{Taxation (一般不考虑)}\\ \text{Liquidity Risk}\\ \text{Credit Risk} \end{array}\right.\\ + &&+\\ \text{Benchmark} &=& \text{Risk-free rate of Return} & \left\{\begin{array}{l} \text{Expectted Inflation Rate}\\ \text{Expected Real Rate} \end{array}\right.\\ \end{array} \]

  • Benchmark spread

    • G-Spread (只考虑 Liquidity 和 Credit risk 的风险补偿)

      • \(\text{YTM}_C-\text{YTM}_T\)

      • 公司债券Yield-国债Yield

      • 同期限

    • Z-Spread (只考虑Liquidity 和 Credit risk的风险补偿)

      • \(SR_c - SR_T\)

      • 公司债券Spot rate-国债Spot rate

    • I-Spread

      • 比较的是Swap rate

    • 各种Spread区别是用的收益率不同

    • Option-adjusted spread (OAS) 不考虑option的Z-spread

      • Callable bond: OAS < Z-Spread

      • Puttable bond: OAS>Z-Spread

      • 跟前边OAY很相似 \[ \text{Callable bond}\\ \begin{array}{rcl}\hline \text{G/Z-Spread}\left\{ \begin{array}{lll} \text{Option Risk}\\ \text{OSA}\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \text{Liquidity risk}\\ \text{Credit risk} \end{array}\right. \end{array} \right.\\\hline \end{array}\\ \text{Treasury (Risk-Free)} \]

1. Securitization

Process

\[ \text{Borrower}\quad \begin{array}{c} \displaystyle\operatorname*\longrightarrow^\text{抵押}\\ \displaystyle\operatorname*\longleftarrow_\text{\$} \end{array}\quad \text{商业银行} \begin{array}{c} \displaystyle\operatorname*\longrightarrow^\text{Sell the Loan}\\ \displaystyle\operatorname*\longleftarrow_\$ \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{c} \text{SPV\SPE}\\ \text{Bankruptcy-remote}\\ \text{(破产隔离)} \end{array}\quad \begin{array}{c} \displaystyle\operatorname*\longrightarrow^\text{打包\分层MBS}\\ \displaystyle\operatorname*\longleftarrow_\text{\$} \end{array}\quad \text{Investor} \]

Main Party

  • SPV

  • Bank (Originator)

  • 其他都是Third Party

Advantages

  • Remove the wall between the borrowers and investors
  • Reduce the liquidity risk
  • Innovations

Structures of Securitizations

  • Credit Tranching
    • Share losses resulting from defaults
  • Time Tranching
    • Posses different expected maturities

2. Mortgage-Backed Securities

ABS and CDO Structure

\[ \left\{\begin{array}{l} \text{ABS} \left\{\begin{array}{l} \text{MBS} \left\{\begin{array}{l} \text{Residual MBS} \left\{\begin{array}{l} \text{Agency RMBS (SPE\SPC 发行的高质量的)}\\ \text{Non-Agency RMBS (Hight Risk，Need credit enhancement)} \end{array}\right. \\ \text{Commericial MBS} \end{array}\right.\\ \text{Non-Mortgage ABS} \end{array}\right.\\ \text{CDO} \end{array}\right. \]

Concepts in Residual Mortgage Loans

  • Loan-to-value ratio (LTV)
    • \(\frac{贷款}{抵押价值}\)
    • The higher LTV, the lower the borrower’s equity
  • Foreclosure
    • 如果违约丧失对抵押物的赎回权
  • Recourse loan
    • 有追索权，房子卖掉还不够，还要继续赔偿
  • Nonrecourse loan
    • 无追索权
  • Strategic default
    • 当房子价值小于贷款余额

Concepts in Residual MBS

  • Conforming mortgage
    • 高质量贷款人群
  • Non-conforming mortgage
    • 低质量贷款人群
  • MBS 分为 MPS 和 CMO
    • Mortgage passthrough securities (MPS)
      • Mortgages形成一个 pool，SPE直接打包卖掉
      • Pass-through rate
        • 是MPS的coupon rate
        • Pass-through rate = mortgage rate - servicing and other fees
    • Collateralized Mortgage Obligations (CMO)
      • 需要分层
  • Weighted average maturity (WAM) 加权平均到期日
  • Weighted average coupon (WAC) 加权平均利率

Prepayment Risk

  • 与scheduled cash flow不同的部分

  • 分为

    • Contraction Risk 利率下降的时候，还的多
    • Extension Risk 利率上升的时候，还的少

  • Prepayment rates

    • Conditional prepayment rate (CPR)

      • is the annual rate

    • Public Securities Association Prepayment Benchmark (PSA)

      • 100% PSA是CPR=0.2%从第一个月开始到第30个月，每月增长0.2%
      • 160% PSA就是所有数据×1.6

    • Single monthly mortality rate (SMM) \[ =\frac{\text{每个月多还的金额}}{\text{期初余额-本来应该还的本月本金}}\\ \]

      • Relationship between SMM and CPR \[ 一年后剩余本金余额=\\1-CPR=(1-SMM)^{12} \]

3. Structure of CMO

  • Reallocation does not eliminate prepayment risk (contraction risk and extension risk)

Sequential Pay Tranches

\[ \begin{array}{rl} A&\text{最大的 Contraction Risk}\\ B\\ C&\text{最大的 Extension Risk} \end{array} \]

  1. 先满足A、B、C的利息
  2. 先还A的本金
  3. A的本金还完后，先偿还B的本金
  4. 现金太少，先满足A的利息

Planned Amortization Class (PAC) CMO

\[ \begin{array}{|c|}\hline \begin{array}{|r|l|}\hline &A\\ \text{PAC}&B\\ &C\\\hline \end{array}\\ \text{Support}\\\hline \end{array} \]

  1. 现金流多，先还Support

  2. 现金流少，不给Support

    PAC有相对稳定现金流

    Support有大的prepayment risk

  3. 现金流太多，Support broken，PAC变成Sequential pay

  4. 现金流太少，PAC也会有Extension Risk

  • PAC拥有稳定现金流的区间叫做initial PAC collar

1. Source of Return

    • 如果持有到期，Annualized HPR \(=\) YTM

    • 如果提前卖掉，价格按照YTM折现，Annualized HRP \(=\) YTM

2. Interest Rate Risk

Definition

  • The price sensitivity to interest rate changes

3. Duration

Investment Horizon Influence

  • Short investment horizon，Market price risk占据主导，YTM上升，Annualized HPR下降
  • Long investment horizon，Reinvestment Risk占据主导，YTM下降，Annualized HPR下降

Macaulay Duration (MacD)

  • 判断Short investment horizon还是Long investment horizon

  • 以折现现金流为权重的现金流平均回流时间

    • \(\text{MacD}=\sum_t \frac{PVCF_t}{P_0}t = \sum_t\frac{PVCF_t}{PVCF_0+PVCF_1+\dotsm+PVCF_T}t\)
      • \(P_0\)是债券价格

  • 如果Investment horizon > MacD，为Long Investment horizon

  • 如果Investment horizon = MacD，无论市场r怎么变，对Annualized HRP都没有影响

  • Duration Gap

    • Duration Gap = MacD - Investment horizon
    • Duration gap > 0
      • Short investment horizon

  • Example

    An investor purchases a 30-year, 5% annual pay bond at 86.24 and plans to sell it in 11 years. Immediately after purchase, interest rates increase by 1%, and they remain at that level until maturity. Assuming coupons are reinvested at the new yield, the investor’s realized horizon yield is closest to

    N=30 PV=-86.24 PMT=5 FV=100 I/Y?=7

    N=11 I/Y=7 PV=0 PMT=5 FV?=78.918

    N=19 I/Y=7 PMT=5 FV=100 PV?=79.3288

    总计收入78.918+79.3288=158.2468

    \(86.24(1+r)^{11}=158.2468\) ? \(r=0.0567\)

Modified Duration

  • Definition

    \[ =\frac{\text{Macaulay Duration}}{1+\text{Periodic market yield}}=-\frac{\Delta P/P_0}{\Delta y} \]

    • 收益率变动1%导致债券价格变动百分比

    • \(\Delta y\) 是yield的变动百分比

    • Derivation \[ P=\frac{CF_1}{(1+r)}+\frac{CF_2}{(1+r)^2}+\frac{CF_3}{(1+r)^3}\\ -\frac{\Delta P}{\Delta r}/P_0=\frac{1}{1+r}\left(\frac{CF_1}{(1+r)}+\frac{2CF_2}{(1+r)^2}+\frac{3CF_3}{(1+r)^3}\right)/P_0\\ =\frac{\text{Macaulay Duration}}{1+\text{Periodic market yield}} \]

  • Price-Yield Curve

    • 纵轴Price，横轴YTM

    • 某一点的切线的斜率的绝对值就是Modified Duration

    • 当r变化太大时，不准确，所以引入Approximate modified duration and effective Duration

Approximate modified duration and effective duration

\[ \text{Duration} = \frac{V_--V_+}{2V_0\Delta y} \]

  • 如果\(\Delta y\)是YTM的变化，是Approximate modified duration

  • 如果\(\Delta y\)是Benchmark yield curve的变化，是Effective duration

  • 更加适用于option bond，因为option bond yield curve更复杂

  • Durations of all option-free bond are positive

Money Duration (Dollar Duration)

  • 收益率变动1%导致债券价格变动量

  • Money Duration = Modified Duration \(\times\) Bond Full Price

Price Value of a Basis Point (PVBP)

  • PVBP = \(\text{Modified Duration}\times \text{Bond Full Price} \times 1\ bp\) = \(\frac{V_--V_+}{2}\)

  • Overaction effect

    • 烂股票总是比好股票表现好
    • 解释：对好股票的期望高

  • Momentum anomalies

    • 趋势投资，股票价格有惯性
    • 解释：非常短的时间存在，但是长期不可能存在

  • Size-effect

    • 小盘股表现通常比大盘股好
    • 解释：小盘股更容易被操纵

  • Value-effect

    • 价值股通常比成长股好
    • 解释：因为对成长股期望更高

  • closed-end investment funds

    • 封闭式基金二级市场交易价格低
    • 解释：因为流动性差，所以折价

  • Earning announcement

    • 股票价格不随公告调整
    • 解释：因为价格已经在公告前调整完成了

  • Initial public offering

    • IPO之后表现差
    • 解释：因为投行可能定价定高了

  • Economic fundamentals

    • 股票价格走势与基本面相背离
    • 解释：二级市场影响股票价格的因素太多了，政策等等

4. Behavior Finance

  • 研究人的非理性行为

  • Loss aversion

    • 定可不要100收益，也不要100亏损

  • Overconfidence

    • 失败总归功于运气

  • Herding

    • 羊群效应，盲目跟风

  • Information cascades

    • 由于跟风导致买入的人越来越多，价格不断上涨
    • 有自主意识的跟风

  • Representativeness

    • 好公司=好股票
    • 不对，应该判断股票被高估还是低估，与公司基本面无关

  • Mental accounting

    • 把资产分成几块，每一块配置不同策略
    • 导致对资产的态度不同（有的用来养老，有的用来风投）
    • 其实没有不同，本质上是没有区别的

  • Conservatism

    • 人对变化的反应很慢
    • 亏损很多后，牛市来了，不敢买

  • Narrow framing

    • 看似两个完全独立的时间其实是有关联的

  • Disposition effect

    • 投资者对浮盈和浮亏的态度不同

  • Gambler’s effect

    • 赌徒的谬论
    • 猜大小，前十次猜大都错了，下一次会猜小，其实没有区别

2. Discounted Cash Flow Models

Dividend Discount Models

  • Valuing preferred stock

    • 永续年金

  • Valuing common stock

    • Golden Growth Model (GGM)

      • Equation \[ P_0=\frac{D_1}{r_e-g}=\frac{D_0(1+g)}{r_e-g} \]

      • Assumption

        • Dividends grow at a constant rate
        • \(n\rightarrow \infty\)
        • \(r_e>g\)

      • 缺点

        • 对r和g很敏感
        • 不适用于不发股利的股票
        • 增长率\(g\)不可能不变

      • Calculation

        • \(D_1=D_0(1+g)\)
        • \(D_0=NI(1-RR)\)
        • \(r=r_f+\beta(r_m-r_f)\)
        • \(g=ROE\times RR\)

    • Two stage DDM and three state DDM

      • 未来现金流分阶段折现

    • Example

      Assume that the stock is expected to pay dividends at the end of Year 1 and Year 2 of $1.25 and $1.56, respectively. Dividends are expected to grow at 5% rate thereafter. Assuming that \(R_e=11\%\), the value of the stock is closet to ? \[ \frac{1.25}{1+0.11}+\frac{1.56}{(1+0.11)^2}+\frac{1.56(1+0.05)}{(0.11-0.05)(1+0.11)^2}=24.55 \]

Free Cash Flow to Equity models

  • 把上边模型的\(D_1\)换成\(FCFE_1\)，其他完全一样

3. Price Multiple Models

  • How to apply price multiples

    • Price multiples based on fundamentals
      • 找一个基准数值，定量计算价格
    • Price multiples based on comparables
      • 找一个基准，判断高估还是低估

  • Include

    • Price to earnings (PE)

      • Justified PE

        • 分子是Intrinsic value而不是Market value

        • Leading/Forward PE: \[ \frac{P_0}{E_1}=\frac{D_1/E_1}{r-g}=\frac{1-RR_1}{r-g} \]

        • Trailing PE: \[ \frac{P_0}{E_0}=\frac{(1-RR_0)(1+g)}{r-g} \]

    • EV/EBITBA

      • EV
        • Enterprice value is the total company value, not equity
        • Market value of common stock + market value of preferred equity + market value of debt - cash and short-term investments
      • EBITDA
        • EBIT+折旧+摊销
          • Net income + I + T + D + A
          • 后边四个与公司的盈利能力无关
        • Advantage
          • 可以对比杠杆不同的公司
          • 适合对税率不同的企业估值
          • 适合对资金密集型企业估值
          • EBITDA通常是正数

4. Asset-Based Models

  • 对当前情况进行估值

    \(FV_\text{asset}-FV_\text{debt}=FV_\text{equity}\)

    \[ \text{Price} = \frac{FV_\text{equity}}{\text{Stock # }} \]

  • 适用于：

    • 清算时的公司、能源金融公司、或者小公司
    • 不考虑未来增长

  • 不适用于：

    • 有大量无形资产的公司
