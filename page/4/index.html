1. Balance Sheet (B/S)

Format

$$ \[\begin{array}{c|c} \begin{array}{c} \textbf{Assets} \\ \text{*Cash 现金流量表}\\ （以上是资金的存在形式） \end{array} & \begin{array}{c} \textbf{Liabilities} \end{array} \\& \begin{array}{c} \hline \textbf{Equity}\\ \text{Owner's Contribution}\\ \text{*Retained Earnings 利润表}\\ （以上是资金的来源） \end{array} \end{array}\]

$$

Features

  • B/S表示主要的，剩下的I/S和C/F是补充B/S

  • 是存量表，衡量的是某一个时间点的经营状况

  • 会计恒等式 Asset = Liability + Equity

  • Equity 也可以叫做 Net assets 或者 Residual interest，因为 E = A - L，但是L = A - E不对

  • Double Entry 复试记账，每笔交易或者业务至少影响两个账户

  • Accrual Basis 权责发生制，必须满足以下两个条件

    • 权利和义务已经发生转移

    • 收回款项可以确信

Four Accounts

  • 卖东西，货已发，钱未收

    Equity: R/E+,

    Asset: Account Receivable (A/R)+

    钱到账

    Asset: A/R-

    Asset: Cash+

  • 买东西，货已拿，钱未付

    Asset: Inventory+

    liability: Account Payable+

    付钱

    liability: Account Payable-

    Asset: cash-

  • 卖订阅杂志

    Asset: Cash+

    Liability: Unearned Revenue+

    寄出杂志

    Liability: Unearned Revenue-

    Equity: R/E+

  • 买订阅杂志

    Asset: Cash-

    Asset: Prepaid Expense+

    收到杂志

    Asset: Prepaid Expense-

    Equity: R/E-

Reading 21 Understanding Income Statement

1. Components and Format

Net Income Components

  • Recurring item:
    • +/- Revenues (Ordinary expenses) +/- Other incomes (Other expenses) +/- Gains (Losses)
  • Non-recurring
    • +/- Gain (Loss) from unusual or infrequent items
    • +/- Gain (Loss) from discontinued operation
  • 前2行是Continuing operation，第3行是Discontinuing operation
  • Revenue/Ordinary expense 主营业务
  • Other incomes/Other expenses 其他业务
  • Gain/Loss 炒股等

Format

\[ \begin{array}{l} \text{Revenue 主营业务收入}\\ -\text{COGS 主营业务成本 Cost of goods sold}\\\hline \text{Gross Profits}\\ -\text{SG&A (selling, general, admin)}\\\hline \text{Operating income}\\ +/- \text{Other income (Expense)}\\ +/- \text{Gain (Loss)}\\ +/- \text{Gain (Loss) from unusual or infrequent items}\\\hline \text{EBIT}\\ -\text{Interest}\\ -\text{Tax}\\\hline \text{NI from continuing operaton}\\ +/-\text{gain (loss) from discoutinued operation}\\\hline \text{NI} \end{array} \]

Unusual or Infrequent Items

  • Impairments
    • 减值
  • Restructuring costs
    • 重组费用
  • G/L from the sales of assets
    • 卖设备
  • Changes in accounting policies are to be reported retrospectively

Discontinued Operation Items

  • 卖分公司，决策后到执行前得到的收入 (phaseout period)
  • 卖分公司所得的收入属于Unusual or infrequent items

2. General Principles of Revenue/Expense Recognition

Revenue Recognition

  • Accrual method of accounting

Expense Recognition

  • Matching Principle (费用的确认要匹配收入)
    • COGS 确认主营业务收入同时结转已售商品成本
    • Depreciation and amortization 确认收入时折旧
    • Doubtful account R/E直接减掉有问题账户
    • Warranties 根据历史经验估计保修费用
  • Period Expenses
    • 不能被匹配的费用
    • 记录在某一期间内
    • 比如行政管理费用

Aggressive Accounting or Conservative Accounting

  • Aggressive
    • 过早确认收入和过晚的确认费用
  • Conservative
    • 过晚确认收入和过早的确认费用
  • 前提是都要符合会计准则
  • 大部分企业都Aggressive

3. Five Steps in Recognizing Revenue

  • Identify the contract with consumer
  • Identify the obligation in the contract
  • Determine the transaction price
  • Allocate the transaction price to the performance obligation in the contract
  • Recognize revenue

4. Basic Earnings Per Share (EPS)

  • 严格定义为每一股普通股股份所对应的利润，所以应该把 Preferred stock 减掉

    \[ EPS=\frac{NI-DIV_{\text{pref. stock}}}{\text{Weighted average number of common shares outstanding}} \]

    • 增发（New issue）和回购（Repurchase）加权平均
    • 拆股和并股（2 for 1，1拆2；1 for 2，2并1），不做加权平均，对之前进行追溯处理
    • Stock dividend，相当于拆股，比如10% stock dividend，则相当于10股拆11股

5. Diluted Earnings Per Share

  • 复杂的资本结构需要计算Diluted EPS

    • Convertible preferred stock
    • Convertible debt
    • Stock options / warrants

  • 如果行使权力后的EPS比Basic大，叫做Anti-dilutive，则Diluted EPS就等于Basic EPS

  • Diluted EPS cannot be higher than the basic EPS

  • 要计算Basic EPS，取小的为Diluted EPS

  • Convertible preferred stock

    \[ \text{Diluted EPS}=\frac{NI-DIV_{\text{Pref.}}+DIV_{\text{Conv. Pref.}}}{\text{Weighted average shares} + \text{Shares form conversion of conv. pref.}} \]

  • Convertible debt

    \[ \text{Diluted EPS} = \frac{NI-DIV_{\text{Pref.}}+\text{Conv. Debt Interest}(1-t)}{\text{Weighted average shares} +\text{Shares from conversion of conv. debt}} \]

  • Stock options / warrants

    \[ \text{Diluted EPS}=\frac{NI-DIV_\text{Pref.}}{\text{Weighted average shares} + \text{库存股}} \]

    • 库存股(Treasury stock) = 发出股票-收到钱/股票全年平均价格

      • 本质：当公司收到期权人行权后会拿行权人给的钱去市场上回购股票

6. Analysis of Income Statement

7. Comprehensive Income

Where is CI

  • OCI可以放在利润表后边，也可以单独成表

CI Includes

  • NI + OCI = CI

OCI Components

2. Direct Method for Calculating CFO

  • + Cash from customer
    • Beginning A/R + Revenue - Cash received = Ending A/R
  • - Cash paid to suppliers
    • Beginning A/P + Purchase - Cash paid = Ending A/P
    • Beginning Inventory + Purchase - COGS = Ending Inventory
  • - Cash paid to employees
    • Beginning wage payable + Wage expense - Cash paid = Ending wage payable
  • - Interest paid
    • Beginning interest payable + Interest expense - Cash paid = Ending interest payable
  • - Taxes paid
    • Beginning tax payable + Taxable income \(\times\) Tax rate - Cash paid = Ending tax payable
    • 所得税费用(tax expense) = 应纳所得税(taxable income \(\times\) tax rate) + \(\Delta DTL\) - \(\Delta DTA\)

3. Indirect Method for Calculating CFO

  • Net income
    • Cash = E + L - \(A'\)
  • I/S
    • + None cash charge (Depreciation)
    • +/- None operating items (Gain from sailing old machine)
  • B/S
    • 长期资产和负债与CFO无关
    • - Increase in current asset accounts (A/R, Inventory)
    • + Increase in current liability accounts (A/P, Tax payable)

4. Direct Method for Calculating CFI

  • 买卖PPE：

    CFI = Inflow（Selling price）- Outflow（Purchase）

    • Selling price= G/L + \(NBV_{\text{disposal}}\)

      \(NBV_{\text{disposal}}\) = Gross disposal - Acc. Depreciation

    • \(NBV_{\text{total, begin}} + \text{Purchase} - \text{S} = NBV_{\text{total, end}}\)

      • S:

        \(NBV_{\text{disposal}}\)

        Depreciation expense

        Impairment （基本不考 ）

    CFI = G/L - Depreciation expense - $NBV_{} $

5. Direct Method for Calculating CFF

  • Review long-term debt and stock
  • Dividend paid
    • Begin dividend payable + Dividend declared - Dividend paid = Ending dividend payable
    • Begin R/E + Net income - dividend declared = End R/E

6. Analysis of Cash Flow

  • Common size analysis

    \[ \frac{\text{Cash flow}}{\text{Revenue}} \] \[ \frac{\text{Cash inflow}}{\text{Total cash inflow}} \]

    \[ \frac{\text{Cash outflow}}{\text{Total cash outflow}} \]

7. Free Cash Flow

  • 能够自由使用的Cash flow

  • Free cash flow to firm (FCFF)

    • FCFF =NI + None cash charge (depreciation) - WC Inv. - FC Inv. + [Interest \(\times\) (1-tax rate)]
      • CA - CL = WC
      • FCFF = CFO - FC Inv. + [Interest \(\times\) (1-tax rate)]

  • Free cash flow to equity (FCFE)

    • FCFE = FCFF - [interest \(\times\) (1-tax rate)] + Net debt borrowing
      • FCFE = CFO - FC Inv. + Net debt borrowing

Reading 24 Financial Analysis Techniques

1. Structure

  • Ratio
    • Activity ratio
    • Liquidity ratio
    • Solvency ratio
    • Profitability ratio
      • Dupont Analysis
  • Other issues

2. Activity Ratio

  • 一般情况 xx turnover = Revenue / Average xx

  • Inventory turnover = COGS / Average inventory

    • Days of inventory on hand (DOH) = 365 / Inventory turnover

  • Receivable turnover = Revenue / Average receivable

    • Days of sales outstanding (DSO) = 365 / Receivable turnover

  • Payable turnover = Purchase / Average payable

    • Begin Inventory + Purchase - COGS = End Inventory
    • Number of days of payable = 365 / Payable turnover

  • Total asset turnover =Revenue / Average total asset

  • Fix asset turnover = Revenue / Average net fixed asset

  • Working capital turnover = Revenue / Average working capital

    • working capital = CA - CL

  • Operating circle = DOH + DSO

  • Cash conversion cycle (Net operating circle) = DOH + DSO - Number of days of payable

3. Liquidity Ratio

  • Current ratio = Current assets / Current liabilities
  • Quick ratio = (Cash + Short-term marketable security + Receivable) / Current liabilities
    • 速动比率
    • 拿掉了inventory
  • Cash ratio = (Cash + Short-term marketable security) / Current liabilities
    • 现金比率
  • Defensive interval = (Cash + Short-term marketable security + Receivable) / Daily cash expenditures

4. Solvency Ratio

  • Debt-to-equity ratio = Total debt / Total equity

  • Debt-to-capital ratio = Total debt / (Total debt + Total equity)

    • 去掉了 Non-debt 的 Liability
    • 不是Equity里的的capital（股本）
    • 这里是资本的意思

  • Debt-to-asset ratio

  • Financial leverage = Average total asset / Average total equity

  • Coverage ratio

    • Interest coverage = EBIT / Interest
    • Fixed charge coverage = (EBIT + Lease payments) / (Interest + Lease payments)
      • Lease payments 每年必须支出的部分

5. Profitability Ratio

  • I/S items

    • Gross profit margin = Gross profit / Net revenue
      • 毛利率
    • Operating profit margin = EBIT / Net revenue
    • Pretax margin = EBT / Net revenue
    • Net profit margin = Net income / Net revenue
      • 净利率

  • B/S items

    • Return on asset (ROA) = NI / Average total assets
      • Operating ROA = EBIT / Average total assets
    • Return on equity (ROE) = NI / Average total equity

  • DuPoint Analysis

    • Two parts \[ ROE=\frac{\text{Net income}}{\text{Total asset}}\times \frac{\text{Total asset}}{\text{Net income}}=ROA\times \text{Financial Leverage} \]

    • Three parts \[ ROE=\frac{\text{Net income}}{\text{Revenue}}\times \frac{\text{Revenue}}{\text{Total asset}}\times \frac{\text{Total asset}}{\text{Net income}}\\=\text{Net profit margin}\times \text{Asset turnover}\times \text{Financial leverage} \]

    • Five parts \[ ROE=\frac{\text{Net income}}{EBT}\times \frac{EBT}{EBIT}\times\frac{EBIT}{\text{Revenue}}\times \frac{\text{Revenue}}{\text{Total asset}}\times \frac{\text{Total asset}}{\text{Net income}}\\ =\text{Tax burden }(1-t)\times \text{Interest burden }(1-t') \times \text{Operating profit margin} \times \text{Asset turnover}\times \text{Financial Leverage} \]

6. Equity, Credit Analysis and Segment Analysis

  • Equity Analysis
    • Dividend payout ratio = Dividend / Net income
    • Retention ratio = 1 - Dividend Payout ratio
    • Sustainable growth rate (g) = ROE \(\times\) Retention ratio
  • Credit Analysis
    • Z - score
      • Z < 1.81 可能破产
  • Segment reporting
    • 如果有一个分公司 Revenue, Asset 或者 Profit 超过10%，需要在Footnotes里披露

3. Economics

Reading 12 Topics in Demand and Supply Analysis

1. Demand Function

  • \(Q_D^{X}=f(P_X,I,P_Y)\)
    • \(P_X\) denotes the price of goods \(X\)
    • 负系数
    • \(I\) denotes the income
    • 正系数：Normal goods
    • 负系数：Inferior goods
    • \(P_Y\) denotes the price of goods \(Y\)
      • 正系数：替代品
      • 负系数：互补品

2. Supply Function

  • \(Q_S^X = f(P_X,W,P_Y)\)
    • \(P_X\) denotes the price of goods \(X\)
      • 正系数
    • \(W\) denotes the wage
      • 负系数
    • \(P_Y\) denotes the price of 原料
      • 负系数

3. Movements and Shifts

  • Price change
    • Movements along demand and supply curves
  • Other factors change
    • Shifts in demand and supply curves

4. Equilibrium, Surplus and Deadweight Loss

  • \(P^*\): Equilibrium price

  • \(Q^*\): Equilibrium quantity

  • CS: Consumer Surplus

    • 支付意愿的价格-实际价格

  • PS: Producer Surplus

    • 实际价格-成本

  • Total Surplus = CS+PS

    • 就是社会福利

  • Deadweight Loss

    • 生产过量
    • 生产不足

5. Elasticity

Price elasticity

  • \(E_{P_X}=\frac{\Delta Q/Q}{\Delta P/P}=\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta P}\frac{P}{Q}\)
    • 大于1，弹性大
    • 小于1，弹性小
  • 垂直线：Perfectly inelastic
    • 刚需（胰岛素）
  • 水平线：Perfectly elastic
  • Unitary elasticity：弹性等于1的点

  • 弹性等于1，Revenue最高
    • Revenue = PQ
    • 弹性大，降价，Q变动比P快
    • 弹性小，涨价，P变动比Q快

Income Elasticity

  • Positive: Normal good
    • 大于1：Luxury good
    • 小于1：Necessities
  • Negative: Inferior good

Cross Elasticity

  • Negative：互补品
  • Positive：替代品
  • 计算相同

6. Substitution Effect and Income Effect

Substitution effect

  • Price下降，数量上升

Income effect

  • Price下降，相当于收入提高
    • 如果是normal goods，则数量上升
    • 若果是inferior goods，
      • Normal inferior good: substitution effect强，上升
      • Giffen good, 特殊的inferior good，income effect强，下降（饥荒时期口粮是Giffen商品）

Veblen Goods

  • 高端奢侈品（超跑）

  • 价格越高，数量越大

  • 与Giffen goods相比

    • 不能被经济学解释
    • Veblen是高端奢侈品(conspicuous good，彰显地位的商品)，Giffen是inferior good

7. Profit, Product, Revenue and Costs

Profit Classification

  • Economic profit = abnormal profit = 超额利润

  • Economic profit = accounting profit-implicit costs = total revenue - total economic costs \[ \text{Revenue}\left\{\begin{array}{rl} \text{accounting profit}&\left\{\begin{array}{l}\text{economic profit (abnormal profit)}\\ \text{implicit cost (normal profit)}\end{array}\right.\\ \text{acounting cost} & \text{explicit cost}\\ \end{array}\right. \]

    • Economic cost = implicit cost + explicit cost

    • Implicit cost 包含资金成本和人力成本

Product

  • Key terms
    • Total product (TP)
    • Average product (AP)
      • 单位劳动力的产量
    • Marginal product (MP)
      • 新增带来的新增
  • Law of diminishing marginal returns
    • MP是TP的斜率，MP=0时，TP最大
    • MP>AP，AP 上升
    • MP=AP，AP最大
    • MP<AP，AP减小

Revenue

  • Key terms

    • Total Revenue (TR)
      • \(P\times Q\)
    • Average Revenue (AR)
    • Marginal Revenue (MR)

  • Under perfect competition

    • AR=MR=P=D

  • Under imperfect competition

    • \(R=PQ\\ MR=\frac{\Delta P}{\Delta Q}Q+P= P(1+\frac{Q}{P}\frac{\Delta P}{\Delta Q})=P\left[1+\frac{1}{E_p}\right]\)
      • 其中\(E_p\) 是负数

Costs

  • Key terms

    • Short term
      • 有固定成本
    • Long term
      • 没有固定成本

2. Investment-Saving (IS) Curve

Fiscal Balance and Trade Balance

  • (S-I)=(G-T)+(X-M)
    • G-T: Fiscal balance
      • > 0, Government deficit
      • < 0, Government surplus
    • X-M: Trade balance
      • > 0, Trade surplus
      • < 0, Trade deficit

IS Curve Meaning

  • 商品市场均衡

IS Curve Assumption

  • S=I，忽略G-T和X-M

IS Curve Derivation

  • \(S(Y) = I(r)\)

    • S 与 Y 同向关系
    • I 与 Y 同向关系，与r反向关系，但是r对I影响更大

  • 反映了Real Interest和Real GDP在商品市场 (GDP核算的角度) 的关系

Position in IS Curve

  • 在线上I=S
  • 在右上方I<S，供给过剩
  • 在左下方I>S，需求过剩

Movements and Shifts

  • 利率变：Movement along the curve
  • X-M增加或扩张财政政策：Right shift the curve

5. Aggregate Supply (AS) Curve

AS Curve Classification

  • Very short run (VSRAS)

    • 在短期，可以不改变物价，但是产出更多产品（增加工人工作时间）

  • Short run (SRAS)

    • 斜向上的线，物价水平高，Y高

    • 但因为工资有粘性，成本会上升，导致总产出变化减小

  • Long run (LRAS)

    • 一定技术下最大产出量，竖直线

  • LRAS所对应的Real GDP叫做Potential GDP，是在full employment下的GDP

  • Full employment定义为在Natural rate of unemployment下的就业

Shifts

  • 对长期和短期的共同影响：人力，资本（自然资源，机器等），科技
  • 只影响短期的（影响成本的因素）：
    • 就是企业的生产意愿，比如工资，能源价格，预计未来价格，补贴，税收，汇率

6. Macroeconomic Equilibrium

  • Long-run equilibrium
    • AD线对长期的Y (Potential GDP)没有影响
  • Recession
    • Decrease in AD
    • Do nothing
    • 需求下降，产量降低，价格降低
      • 工资下降，成本下降，供给增加
    • Fiscal and monetary policy
      • 扩张政策将AD推回去
  • Inflation
    • Increase in AD
    • Do nothing 同上
    • Fiscal and monetary policy
      • 紧缩政策将AD推回去
  • Stagflation（滞胀）:

    • 经济停滞，通货膨胀发生
    • Do nothing

  • Decrease in input price

    • 好事，更高的GDP，更低的价格

7. Economic Growth

Factors Influencing Economic Growth

  • 劳动力：Labor and human capital

  • 资本：Physical capital stock and nature resources

  • 科技：Technology

Cobb-Douglas Production Function

  • \(Y=A\times f(L,K)=AK^\alpha L^{1-\alpha}\)

    • Y: Aggregate economic output
    • A: Total factor productivity 全要素生产力（科技）
    • L: Size of labor force
    • K: Amount of capital

  • \(\log Y = \log A+\alpha \log K+(1-\alpha)\log L\)

    • 求导得到\(\frac{\Delta Y}{Y} = \frac{\Delta A}{A} + \alpha \frac{\Delta K}{K}+(1-\alpha)\frac{\Delta L}{L}\)

      • Growth in potential GDP = Growth in technology + \(W_L\) (Growth in labor) + \(W_c\) (Growth in capital)

      • 其中，\(W_L\) 和 \(W_c\) 是劳动力和资本在国民收入中share的比例

    • 人均GDP增长

      \[ Y/L = A(K/L)^\alpha\\ \log (Y/L) = \log A+\alpha \log (K/L)\\ \frac{\Delta (Y/L)}{Y/L} = \frac{\Delta A}{A} + \alpha \frac{\Delta(K/L)}{K/L} \]

      • Growth in per-capita potential GDP = Growth in technology + \(W_c\) (Growth in the capital to labor ratio)

Sustainable Growth Model

\[ Y=Y=(Y/L)L\\ \log Y = \log (Y/L)+\log L\\ \frac{\Delta Y}{Y}=\frac{\Delta(Y/L)}{Y/L}+\frac{\Delta L}{L} \] * Growth in potential GDP = Growth in labor productivity + Growth in rate of labor force

As asset values increase, consumers save less and spend more out of current income since they will still be able to meet their wealth accumulation goals. Therefore, an increase in household wealth results in a rightward shift in the aggregate demand curve.
