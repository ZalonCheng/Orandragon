3. Economics

Reading 12 Topics in Demand and Supply Analysis

1. Demand Function

  • \(Q_D^{X}=f(P_X,I,P_Y)\)
    • \(P_X\) denotes the price of goods \(X\)
    • 负系数
    • \(I\) denotes the income
    • 正系数：Normal goods
    • 负系数：Inferior goods
    • \(P_Y\) denotes the price of goods \(Y\)
      • 正系数：替代品
      • 负系数：互补品

2. Supply Function

  • \(Q_S^X = f(P_X,W,P_Y)\)
    • \(P_X\) denotes the price of goods \(X\)
      • 正系数
    • \(W\) denotes the wage
      • 负系数
    • \(P_Y\) denotes the price of 原料
      • 负系数

3. Movements and Shifts

  • Price change
    • Movements along demand and supply curves
  • Other factors change
    • Shifts in demand and supply curves

4. Equilibrium, Surplus and Deadweight Loss

  • \(P^*\): Equilibrium price

  • \(Q^*\): Equilibrium quantity

  • CS: Consumer Surplus

    • 支付意愿的价格-实际价格

  • PS: Producer Surplus

    • 实际价格-成本

  • Total Surplus = CS+PS

    • 就是社会福利

  • Deadweight Loss

    • 生产过量
    • 生产不足

5. Elasticity

Price elasticity

  • \(E_{P_X}=\frac{\Delta Q/Q}{\Delta P/P}=\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta P}\frac{P}{Q}\)
    • 大于1，弹性大
    • 小于1，弹性小
  • 垂直线：Perfectly inelastic
    • 刚需（胰岛素）
  • 水平线：Perfectly elastic
  • Unitary elasticity：弹性等于1的点

  • 弹性等于1，Revenue最高
    • Revenue = PQ
    • 弹性大，降价，Q变动比P快
    • 弹性小，涨价，P变动比Q快

Income Elasticity

  • Positive: Normal good
    • 大于1：Luxury good
    • 小于1：Necessities
  • Negative: Inferior good

Cross Elasticity

  • Negative：互补品
  • Positive：替代品
  • 计算相同

6. Substitution Effect and Income Effect

Substitution effect

  • Price下降，数量上升

Income effect

  • Price下降，相当于收入提高
    • 如果是normal goods，则数量上升
    • 若果是inferior goods，
      • Normal inferior good: substitution effect强，上升
      • Giffen good, 特殊的inferior good，income effect强，下降（饥荒时期口粮是Giffen商品）

Veblen Goods

  • 高端奢侈品（超跑）

  • 价格越高，数量越大

  • 与Giffen goods相比

    • 不能被经济学解释
    • Veblen是高端奢侈品(conspicuous good，彰显地位的商品)，Giffen是inferior good

7. Profit, Product, Revenue and Costs

Profit Classification

  • Economic profit = abnormal profit = 超额利润

  • Economic profit = accounting profit-implicit costs = total revenue - total economic costs \[ \text{Revenue}\left\{\begin{array}{rl} \text{accounting profit}&\left\{\begin{array}{l}\text{economic profit (abnormal profit)}\\ \text{implicit cost (normal profit)}\end{array}\right.\\ \text{acounting cost} & \text{explicit cost}\\ \end{array}\right. \]

    • Economic cost = implicit cost + explicit cost

    • Implicit cost 包含资金成本和人力成本

Product

  • Key terms
    • Total product (TP)
    • Average product (AP)
      • 单位劳动力的产量
    • Marginal product (MP)
      • 新增带来的新增
  • Law of diminishing marginal returns
    • MP是TP的斜率，MP=0时，TP最大
    • MP>AP，AP 上升
    • MP=AP，AP最大
    • MP<AP，AP减小

Revenue

  • Key terms

    • Total Revenue (TR)
      • \(P\times Q\)
    • Average Revenue (AR)
    • Marginal Revenue (MR)

  • Under perfect competition

    • AR=MR=P=D

  • Under imperfect competition

    • \(R=PQ\\ MR=\frac{\Delta P}{\Delta Q}Q+P= P(1+\frac{Q}{P}\frac{\Delta P}{\Delta Q})=P\left[1+\frac{1}{E_p}\right]\)
      • 其中\(E_p\) 是负数

Costs

  • Key terms

    • Short term
      • 有固定成本
    • Long term
      • 没有固定成本
    • Total cost = Total fixed cost (TFC) + Total variable cost (TVC)
    • Average total cost (ATC)
    • Average variable cost (AVC)
    • Marginal Cost (MC)
      • MC和AVC交在AVC最低点
      • MC和ATC交在ATC最低点

  • Profit maximization

  • TR-TC最大

  • MR=MC

Breakeven point and shutdown point

  • Breakeven point

    • AR=ATC
    • 短期stay in market，长期exit market

  • Shutdown point

    • AR=AVC

  • Perfect competition

Economics of Scale and Diseconomies of Scale in Long Run

  • Long-run average total cost curve

Reading 14 Aggregate Output, Prices, and Economic Growth

1. GDP

Definition

  • Total market value of final goods and services produced in a country within a certain period of time

  • Exception

    • Rent of own house
      • 用租金核算
    • Government services
      • 用成本核算

  • GNP是国民概念，GDP是地理概念

Nominal GDP and Real GDP

  • Nominal GDP

    • 今年的量，今年的价格

  • Real GDP

    • 今年的量，过去的价格

  • GDP deflator = Nominal GDP / Real GDP

  • Real GDP (Aggregate Output) = Aggregate Income = Aggregate Expenditure = Y

Expenditure Approach

  • 支出法 (以最终商品计算)

  • Economic Structure

    • Domestic
      • Private sector
        • Household
        • Firm
      • Government
    • Foreign

  • 四部门经济

    • Household
    • Firm
    • Government
    • Rest of world

  • GDP=C+I+G+(X-M) 对应四部门经济

    • C 家庭消费支出
      • 个人金融投资不算，算储蓄，不属于支出行为
    • I 企业投资支出
      • 算金融投资，不算实物投资和存货投资，这些算企业的储蓄
    • G 政府支出
      • 不考虑政府的税收
    • X-M 国际支持

Income Approach

  • 收入法 (以原材料和中间附加值计算)

  • GDP = National income + Capital consumption allowance (CCA) + Statistical discrepancy (SD)

    • 挖铁矿，需要铁锹，铁锹会磨损，所以要把挖出来的铁补 一部分到铁锹上(CCA)，剩下的就是National income

Personal Income

  • National income 减去不属于个人的部分

  • Household disposable income(HDI) = Personal income - Personal taxes

  • HDI = Personal Consumption + Personal Saving

  • MPC和MPS

    • Marginal Propensity to Consume (MPC)
      • 每增加一块钱消费多少
    • Marginal Propensity to Saving
      • 每增加一块钱储蓄多少
    • MPC+MPS = 1

  • GDP = C + S + T

    • C: 家庭消费支出
    • S: Private sector savings
      • = Business savings + Household savings
    • T: Net Tax
      • Tax - 转移支付

Summary

  • Expenditure approach

    • GDP = C+I+G+(X-M)

  • Income approach

    • GDP = National income + CCA + SD
    • GDP = C+S+T

  • I+G+(X-M) = S+T

    • S = I + (G-T) + (X-M)
      • 国内的saving可以给三类人花
        • I: 企业
        • G-T：Fiscal balance 政府
        • X-M: Trade balance 海外
    • G-T = (S-I) - (X-M)
      • S-I 国内的储蓄
      • X-M 国外的钱

2. Investment-Saving (IS) Curve

Fiscal Balance and Trade Balance

  • (S-I)=(G-T)+(X-M)
    • G-T: Fiscal balance
      • > 0, Government deficit
      • < 0, Government surplus
    • X-M: Trade balance
      • > 0, Trade surplus
      • < 0, Trade deficit

IS Curve Meaning

  • 商品市场均衡

IS Curve Assumption

  • S=I，忽略G-T和X-M

IS Curve Derivation

  • \(S(Y) = I(r)\)

    • S 与 Y 同向关系
    • I 与 Y 同向关系，与r反向关系，但是r对I影响更大

  • 反映了Real Interest和Real GDP在商品市场 (GDP核算的角度) 的关系

Position in IS Curve

  • 在线上I=S
  • 在右上方I<S，供给过剩
  • 在左下方I>S，需求过剩

Movements and Shifts

  • 利率变：Movement along the curve
  • X-M增加或扩张财政政策：Right shift the curve

3. Liquidity-Money (LM) Curve

Quantity theory of money

  • MV = PY
    • M: Nominal money supply
    • V: Velocity of money
    • P: Price level
    • Y: Real GDP

Money Neutrality

  • 短时间内V和Y基本不变

  • M增加会导致P增加

  • M/P=Y/V=常数

Money Demand Theory

  • L1 Demand
    • Transaction demand
    • Precautionary demand
    • L1与Y同向关系
  • L2 Demand
    • Speculative demand
    • L2与r反向关系

LM Curve Derivation

  • r上升，L2下降，M/P是不变的，所以L2上升，Y上升

Position in LM Curve

  • 落在线上，需求等于供给
  • 右下方，需求过剩
  • 左上方，供给过剩

Movements and Shifts

  • 利率变：Movement along
  • M/P增加或扩张型货币政策：Right shift

4. Aggregate Demand (AD) Curve

AD Curve Meaning

  • IS与LM相交交点是AD线，反应了P (Price Level)和Y (Real GDP)的关系

Movements and Shifts

  • Price Level 影响 Real GDP变: Move along
  • Shift:
    • Household wealth
    • Fiscal policy 影响IS曲线
    • Monetary policy 影响LM曲线
    • 全球经济等等

5. Aggregate Supply (AS) Curve

AS Curve Classification

  • Very short run (VSRAS)

    • 在短期，可以不改变物价，但是产出更多产品（增加工人工作时间）

    • 产出量不会影响价格，水平线

  • Short run (SRAS)

    • 斜向上的线，物价水平高，Y高

    • 但因为工资有粘性，成本会上升，导致总产出变化减小

  • Long run (LRAS)

    • 一定技术下最大产出量，竖直线

Potential GDP and Full Employment

  • LRAS所对应的Real GDP叫做Potential GDP，是在full employment下的GDP

  • Full employment定义为在Natural rate of unemployment下的就业

Shifts

  • 对长期和短期的共同影响：人力，资本（自然资源，机器等），科技
  • 只影响短期的（影响成本的因素）：
    • 就是企业的生产意愿，比如工资，能源价格，预计未来价格，补贴，税收，汇率

6. Macroeconomic Equilibrium

  • Long-run equilibrium
    • AD线对长期的Y (Potential GDP)没有影响
  • Recession
    • Decrease in AD
    • Do nothing
    • 需求下降，产量降低，价格降低
      • 工资下降，成本下降，供给增加
    • Fiscal and monetary policy
      • 扩张政策将AD推回去
  • Inflation
    • Increase in AD
    • Do nothing 同上
    • Fiscal and monetary policy
      • 紧缩政策将AD推回去
  • Stagflation（滞胀）:
    • Decrease in AS
    • 能源价格上升，供给减少
    • 经济停滞，通货膨胀发生
    • Do nothing
      • AS回到原来位置，有政治压力
    • 政府干预
      • 增加AD，会导致物价上升
  • Decrease in input price
    • 能源价格下降，供给增加
    • 好事，更高的GDP，更低的价格

7. Economic Growth

Factors Influencing Economic Growth

  • 劳动力：Labor and human capital

  • 资本：Physical capital stock and nature resources

  • 科技：Technology

Cobb-Douglas Production Function

  • \(Y=A\times f(L,K)=AK^\alpha L^{1-\alpha}\)

    • Y: Aggregate economic output
    • A: Total factor productivity 全要素生产力（科技）
    • L: Size of labor force
    • K: Amount of capital

  • \(\log Y = \log A+\alpha \log K+(1-\alpha)\log L\)

    • 求导得到\(\frac{\Delta Y}{Y} = \frac{\Delta A}{A} + \alpha \frac{\Delta K}{K}+(1-\alpha)\frac{\Delta L}{L}\)

      • Growth in potential GDP = Growth in technology + \(W_L\) (Growth in labor) + \(W_c\) (Growth in capital)

      • 其中，\(W_L\) 和 \(W_c\) 是劳动力和资本在国民收入中share的比例

    • 人均GDP增长

      \[ Y/L = A(K/L)^\alpha\\ \log (Y/L) = \log A+\alpha \log (K/L)\\ \frac{\Delta (Y/L)}{Y/L} = \frac{\Delta A}{A} + \alpha \frac{\Delta(K/L)}{K/L} \]

      • Growth in per-capita potential GDP = Growth in technology + \(W_c\) (Growth in the capital to labor ratio)

Sustainable Growth Model

\[ Y=Y=(Y/L)L\\ \log Y = \log (Y/L)+\log L\\ \frac{\Delta Y}{Y}=\frac{\Delta(Y/L)}{Y/L}+\frac{\Delta L}{L} \] * Growth in potential GDP = Growth in labor productivity + Growth in rate of labor force

5. Inflation and Indicators

Labor Market Structure

  • Working age population -> labor force(愿意工作的人)->employment(就业的人)

  • 不想工作不叫失业

Unemployment Classification

  • Frictional unemployment 找工作需要时间
  • Structural unemployment 技术进步导致技术不匹配
  • Cyclical unemployment 周期性失业
    • Full employment 是没有 Cyclical unemployment，因为第一个是必然，第二个是好事

Labor Force Participation Rate

  • Labor force / Working age population

Unemployment Rate

  • (Labor force - Employment) / Labor force

  • 不影响Unemployment rate的因素

    • Underemployed 高技术人员做低等工作
    • Discouraged worker 找不到工作所以不找了，放弃了，不算失业
    • Voluntarily unemployed 就不想上班

Inflation, Deflation, Disinflation

  • Deflation 通缩会对经济有负面作用
  • Disinflation 通胀率在下降

Consumer Price Index (CPI) Calculation

  • CPI = Cost of basket at current prices / Cost of basket at base period prices

  • Lapsers index (last)

    • 用过去一篮子计算
    • 问题：高估趋势
      • New goods: 因为新商品价格会变高（手机）
      • Quality changes: 质量变好，价格变高（桌子）
      • Substitution: 新东西替代旧东西，还在计算旧东西（电缆）

  • Paashche index (present)

    • 用当前一篮子计算

  • Fisher index

    • Fisher index = \(\sqrt{I_p\times I_L}\)

Other Inflation Measurement

  • GDP deflator
  • Producer price index (PPI) = Wholesale price index (WPI)
    • 它是CPI的先行指数
  • Headline inflation 所有商品inflation
  • Core inflation 排除食品和能源，因为食品和能源是国际报价，所以排除

Inflation Comes From

  • Cost push inflation
  • Demand pull inflation

      货币供应量增加，经济变好

    • Interest rate spread 利率

      短期利率一般是稳定的，长期利率下降，说明经济变差

    • Index of consumer expectations 信心

    • Average weekly hours 工作时长

    • Vendor performance 交货时长

Reading 16 Monetary and Fiscal Policy

1. Money and Inflation

Fiscal Policy

  • Government spending and taxation

Monetary Policy

  • Expansionary, Contractionary

Functions of Money

  • Medium of exchange
  • Unit of account
  • Store of value

Money Multiplier

  • 1/(reserve requirement)

Narrow Money and Broad Money

  • M1: 流通的现金 + check (活期存款)

  • M2: M1+time deposit (定期存款)

Who Determine Money Supply

  • Money supply is determined by central bank
  • 所以是个竖直线

Fisher Effect

  • Nominal rate = Real rate + Expected Inflation
  • The idea behind is that real rate is relatively stable, and changes in interest rates are driven by changes in expected inflation.
  • If risky, Nominal rate = real rate + expected inflation + risk premium

2. Central bank

Tool of Central Bank

  • Policy rate
  • Reserve requirements
    • 最强烈的
  • Open market operations
    • 使用最多的
    • 买债券，扩张政策
    • 卖债券，紧缩政策

Neutral Interest Rate

  • Real GDP growth + inflation target
  • Policy rate > Neutral rate
    • 紧缩政策
  • Policy rate < Neutral rate
    • 宽松政策

Qualities of Effective Central Bank

  • Independence
    • 不受政府干扰
    • Operation independence and target independence
  • Credible
  • Transparent

3. Monetary Policy Limitations

  • Bond market vigilantes

    投资者理性分析后操作与央行相反

  • Liquidity trap

    在经济危机中，银行不愿贷款，居民企业也不愿借钱，央行增加流动性是无效的

    解决方法：Quantitative easing，政府买债券来影响利率

4. Fiscal Policy

Tools

  • Spending
    • Transfer payments：补贴
    • Current spending：买东西
    • Capital spending：大项目
  • Revenue
    • 直接税：对人课税
    • 间接税：对物课税（影响更快）

Limitations

  • Delay
    • Recognition lag
    • Action lag
    • Impact lag
  • Crowding-out effect
    • 政府扩张政策，政府借钱，政府拉高利率，私营企业无法借钱
  • Ricardian equivalence
    • 政府如果减税，劳动人民看穿会在未来加税，所以大家存钱，财政政策失效

Fiscal Multiplier

  • \(\frac{1}{1-MPC(1-t)}\)
    • t: tax
    • MPC: marginal propensity of consumption 边际消费倾向

Debt Ratio

  • 负债/GDP

Reading 17 International Trade and Capital Flows

1. International Trade Benefits

Comparative Advantage Explanation

  • Ricardian Model
    • Labor is the only factor of production
    • 科技不同造成comparative advantage
  • Heckscher-Ohlin Model
    • Both labor and capital are variable factors of production
    • 各自从事擅长的（中国从事劳动力，美国从事资本）

1. Foreign Exchange Rate

Nominal and Real Exchange Rate

  • 7.1 RMB/USD 是Nominal exchange rate

  • \(\frac{7.1/CPI_{RMB}}{1/CPI_{USD}}\)是Real exchange rate

Direct Quota and Indirect Quota

  • 2 元/苹果，考察对象是苹果，所以A/B的考察对象是B

  • 50 A/B

    • A
      • Price currency
      • Domestic currency
      • Indirect quota
    • B
      • Base currency
      • Foreign currency
      • Direct quota

  • Cross Rate

2. Forward Exchange Rates

Forward Discount or Premium

  • Derivation \[ 1+r_{DC}=\frac{1}{S}(1+r_{FC})\times F\\ \frac{F}{S} = \frac{1+r_{DC}}{1+r_{FC}} \]

  • 所以如果给A/B，则有 \(\frac{F}{S}=\frac{1+r_A}{1+r_B}\)

Interest Rate Parity (IRP)

\[ \frac{F-S}{S} = \frac{r_X-r_Y}{1+r_Y} \approx r_X-r_Y \]

Improve Trade Deficit

  • Elasticity Approach

    • 进出口商品弹性大才有用

    • 所以进出口奢侈品和有替代品的商品有用，必需品没用

    • J-Curve

      • 有滞后性，因为原来是有合同的，所以刚开始会恶化赤字

  • Absorption Approach

    • Y=C+I+G+(X-M)

    • X-M = Y-(C+I+G)

    • C+I+G是本国吸收

  • 希望X-M上升，所以增加Y（如果没有实现完全就业可以实现完全就业）或减少C+I+G，降C,I不好，所以降低G
